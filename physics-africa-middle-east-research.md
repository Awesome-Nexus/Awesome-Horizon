## NOTES

- **URL Verification**: All URLs listed have been verified through web search. However, some institutional websites may change over time.
- **Research Focus**: Many departments have specific research specializations not fully detailed here. Interested students and researchers should visit individual department websites for more detailed information.
- **International Collaborations**: Many of these institutions have active collaborations with international research centers including CERN, ICTP, and various European and American universities.
- **Language of Instruction**: Most North African and Middle Eastern institutions use English or French as the primary language of instruction, while some use Arabic or local languages alongside English.
