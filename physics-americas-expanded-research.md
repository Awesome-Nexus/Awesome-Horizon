## Summary by Region

### Central America Summary
- **Total Countries with Physics Programs**: 7
- **Key Institutions**: UCR (Costa Rica), UNAH (Honduras), UES (El Salvador), USAC (Guatemala), UB (Belize)
- **Regional Challenge**: Limited research infrastructure; focus primarily on undergraduate education and teacher training

### Caribbean Summary
- **Total Countries with Physics Programs**: 12+
- **Key Institutions**: UWI (three campuses: Jamaica, Trinidad, Barbados), UPR (Puerto Rico), UASD (Dominican Republic)
- **Regional Organizations**: Caribbean Academy of Sciences
- **Note**: Physics education heavily concentrated in UWI system

### South America Summary
- **Total Countries with Physics Programs**: 10
- **Leading Countries**: Colombia, Peru, Venezuela (strong research traditions)
- **Key Regional Resources**: CLAF, FeLaSoFi, LA-CoNGA Physics
- **Emerging Programs**: Ecuador (USFQ), Bolivia, Paraguay, Uruguay
