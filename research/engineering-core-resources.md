## 1. MAJOR ENGINEERING DISCIPLINES

### Mechanical Engineering
**Description:** One of the broadest engineering disciplines, dealing with the design, analysis, manufacturing, and maintenance of mechanical systems.  
**Key Areas:** Thermodynamics, fluid mechanics, structural analysis, robotics, automotive engineering, HVAC  
**Applications:** Vehicles, aircraft, industrial machinery, energy systems, manufacturing equipment  
**Professional Organization:** ASME (American Society of Mechanical Engineers)

### Electrical/Electronics Engineering
**Description:** Engineering focused on electricity, electronics, and electromagnetism.  
**Key Areas:** Power systems, control systems, telecommunications, signal processing, microelectronics  
**Applications:** Power grids, electric vehicles, telecommunications, consumer electronics, renewable energy  
**Professional Organization:** IEEE (Institute of Electrical and Electronics Engineers)

### Civil Engineering
**Description:** The design, construction, and maintenance of the physical and naturally built environment.  
**Key Areas:** Structural engineering, geotechnical engineering, transportation, water resources, environmental  
**Applications:** Bridges, buildings, dams, roads, tunnels, water supply systems  
**Professional Organization:** ASCE (American Society of Civil Engineers)

### Chemical Engineering
**Description:** Application of physical sciences and life sciences to convert raw materials into valuable products.  
**Key Areas:** Process design, thermodynamics, transport phenomena, reaction engineering, materials  
**Applications:** Pharmaceuticals, petrochemicals, food processing, energy, biotechnology  
**Professional Organization:** AIChE (American Institute of Chemical Engineers)

### Computer Engineering
**Description:** Integration of computer science and electrical engineering to develop computer hardware and software.  
**Key Areas:** Computer architecture, embedded systems, VLSI design, networking, robotics  
**Applications:** Microprocessors, computer systems, embedded devices, IoT systems  
**Professional Organization:** IEEE Computer Society, ACM

### Aerospace/Aeronautical Engineering
**Description:** Design and development of aircraft, spacecraft, and related systems.  
**Key Areas:** Aerodynamics, propulsion, structures, avionics, orbital mechanics  
**Applications:** Commercial aircraft, satellites, rockets, defense systems, drones  
**Professional Organizations:** AIAA (American Institute of Aeronautics and Astronautics), RAeS (Royal Aeronautical Society)

### Biomedical Engineering
**Description:** Application of engineering principles to medicine and biology for healthcare purposes.  
**Key Areas:** Medical devices, biomaterials, tissue engineering, biomechanics, neural engineering  
**Applications:** Prosthetics, imaging systems, artificial organs, diagnostic equipment  
**Professional Organization:** IEEE EMBS, BMES (Biomedical Engineering Society)

### Industrial Engineering
**Description:** Optimization of systems, processes, and efficiency in production and operations.  
**Key Areas:** Operations research, supply chain management, ergonomics, quality control, systems engineering  
**Applications:** Manufacturing optimization, logistics, healthcare systems, financial services  
**Professional Organization:** IISE (Institute of Industrial and Systems Engineers)

### Environmental Engineering
**Description:** Application of science and engineering principles to improve and maintain the environment.  
**Key Areas:** Water treatment, air quality, waste management, pollution control, sustainability  
**Applications:** Wastewater treatment, environmental remediation, sustainable design  
**Professional Organization:** AAEE (American Academy of Environmental Engineers and Scientists)

### Materials Science/Engineering
**Description:** Study of the properties of matter and their applications to various areas of science and engineering.  
**Key Areas:** Metallurgy, ceramics, polymers, composites, nanomaterials, biomaterials  
**Applications:** Advanced materials for aerospace, electronics, medical devices, construction  
**Professional Organization:** TMS (The Minerals, Metals & Materials Society)

### Petroleum Engineering
**Description:** Concerned with the production of hydrocarbons (oil and natural gas).  
**Key Areas:** Reservoir engineering, drilling, production, petrophysics, formation evaluation  
**Applications:** Oil and gas extraction, unconventional resources, enhanced oil recovery  
**Professional Organization:** SPE (Society of Petroleum Engineers)

### Nuclear Engineering
**Description:** Application of nuclear processes for energy production and other applications.  
**Key Areas:** Reactor physics, radiation protection, nuclear fuel cycle, fusion energy  
**Applications:** Nuclear power plants, medical isotopes, nuclear medicine, research reactors  
**Professional Organization:** ANS (American Nuclear Society)

### Software Engineering
**Description:** Systematic approach to the design, development, operation, and maintenance of software.  
**Key Areas:** Software design, testing, project management, DevOps, security  
**Applications:** Enterprise software, mobile apps, embedded systems, AI/ML systems  
**Professional Organization:** ACM, IEEE Computer Society

- 2.003 Engineering Dynamics
- 6.006 Introduction to Algorithms
