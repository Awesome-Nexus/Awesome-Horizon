# Chinese Academy of Sciences (CAS) Physics Institutes and Facilities

## Overview

The **Chinese Academy of Sciences (CAS)** (中国科学院) is the national academy for natural sciences and the highest consultancy for science and technology of the People's Republic of China. It is the world's largest research organization.

| Attribute | Details |
|-----------|---------|
| **Established** | November 1, 1949 |
| **Headquarters** | 52 Sanlihe Rd, Xicheng, Beijing, China |
| **President** | Hou Jianguo |
| **Staff** | 71,300 (2023) |
| **Research Institutes** | 106 |
| **Universities** | 2 (USTC, UCAS) |
| **Graduate Students** | 79,000 |
| **Budget** | CN¥171 billion (2023) |
| **Official Website** | [http://www.cas.cn](http://www.cas.cn) |

## University of Chinese Academy of Sciences (UCAS) | 中国科学院大学

| Attribute | Details |
|-----------|---------|
| **Established** | 1978 (as Graduate School), 2012 (as UCAS) |
| **Location** | Beijing (multiple campuses) |
| **President** | Prof. Zhou Qi |
| **Students** | ~58,000 (including ~24,000 PhD students) |
| **Website** | [https://english.ucas.ac.cn/](https://english.ucas.ac.cn/) |
| **Chinese Website** | [https://www.ucas.ac.cn/](https://www.ucas.ac.cn/) |

**Overview:**
UCAS is the primary higher education institution of CAS, formed from the merger of the Graduate School of CAS. It integrates education with research, with students conducting research at various CAS institutes.

**Campuses:**
- Yanqihu Campus (main, Huairou District)
- Zhongguancun Campus (Haidian District)
- Yuquanlu Campus (Shijingshan District)

**Key Statistics:**
- More than 30,000 faculty members (including ~15,000 graduate supervisors)
- Partnership with 116 CAS research institutes
- Consistently ranked top 3 globally in Nature Index
