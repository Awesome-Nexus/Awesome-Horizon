# Comprehensive Guide to Biology Research Databases, Journals & Literature Resources

A deep research compilation of major biology publication venues, academic databases, preprint servers, bioinformatics resources, and research tools.

## 1. MAJOR LITERATURE DATABASES

### 1.1 PubMed/MEDLINE
**Website:** https://pubmed.ncbi.nlm.nih.gov/

The premier database for biomedical literature maintained by NCBI/NLM.

| Attribute | Details |
|-----------|---------|
| **Coverage** | 35+ million citations |
| **Content** | Biomedical, life sciences, behavioral sciences |
| **Time Range** | 1946-present (selective coverage to 1809) |
| **Update Frequency** | Daily |
| **Access** | Free |

**Features:**
- MeSH (Medical Subject Headings) controlled vocabulary
- LinkOut to full-text articles
- Clinical queries filter
- Citation matching tool
- My NCBI personal account for saving searches

**Related Resources:**
- **PubMed Central (PMC):** https://www.ncbi.nlm.nih.gov/pmc/ - Free full-text archive (10+ million articles)
- **MEDLINE:** Primary component of PubMed with indexed journals
- **Bookshelf:** https://www.ncbi.nlm.nih.gov/books/ - Biomedical books and documents
