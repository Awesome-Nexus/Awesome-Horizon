# Physics Institutions in Central Asian Countries

## Overview

This document provides comprehensive information about physics institutions in five Central Asian countries: Kazakhstan, Uzbekistan, Turkmenistan, Tajikistan, and Kyrgyzstan. The information covers national physics societies, academies of sciences, university physics departments, research institutes, notable research facilities, international collaborations, and key physicists.
