**Parkes Observatory (Murriyang):**
- Operational since 1961
- 64m diameter dish
- First quasar identified using Parkes data (3C 273)
- Discovered two-thirds of known 1,800 pulsars
- Played key role in Apollo 11 moon landing broadcast
- Listed on Australian National Heritage Register (2020)
- Wiradjuri name: Murriyang (meaning "Sky World")

## 6. Physics Research Areas of Strength

Australia has established internationally recognized excellence across multiple physics disciplines. The following represent key research strengths:

### Quantum Science and Technology

Australia is a world leader in quantum research, with particular strengths in:

| Area | Description | Leading Institutions |
|------|-------------|---------------------|
| **Quantum Computing** | Silicon-based qubits; atomic-scale device fabrication | UNSW (Michelle Simmons), UQ, ANU |
| **Quantum Optics** | Quantum information; entanglement; squeezed light | ANU, UQ |
| **Quantum Sensing** | Precision measurement; atomic clocks; gravimetry | UWA, ANU |
| **Quantum Materials** | Topological materials; quantum dots | University of Melbourne, UNSW |

**Key Initiatives:**
- **Sydney Quantum Academy** - Partnership of Macquarie, UNSW, UTS, and University of Sydney
- **Silicon Quantum Computing** - First company to manufacture quantum computers at atomic scale
- **ARC Centre of Excellence for Quantum Computation and Communication Technology** (CQCT)

### Square Kilometre Array (SKA)

| Attribute | Details |
|-----------|---------|
| **Type** | International mega-science project |
| **Australian Role** | Host of SKA-Low telescope |
| **Location** | Murchison Radio-astronomy Observatory, Western Australia |
| **Partner Country** | South Africa (hosts SKA-Mid) |
| **Australian Institutions** | CSIRO, ICRAR, all major universities |
| **Construction Start** | December 2022 |
| **First Light Expected** | Late 2020s |

**SKA-Low Specifications:**
- 131,072 Christmas tree-shaped antennas
- Receives low-frequency radio waves (50-350 MHz)
- Spans 65 km baseline
- Will generate ~1 Terabyte per second of raw data
- Located on Wajarri Yamaji country (traditional name: Inyarrimanha Ilgari Bundara - "sharing the sky and stars")

**Australian Companies Involved:**
- Ventia (site-wide power and fiber infrastructure)
- SIRIO (antenna manufacturing - Italian-Australian partnership)
- Local WA businesses (training, employment for Wajarri Yamaji)
