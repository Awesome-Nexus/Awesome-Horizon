# Physics Institutions in Burundi

## Overview

Burundi has a developing physics research and education landscape centered primarily around the University of Burundi (Université du Burundi), the country's flagship public university. Physics education and research are concentrated in the Faculty of Science at the University of Burundi, with additional physics education programs at the Institute of Applied Pedagogy. The country has several private universities that offer science and technology programs with physics components.

## 6. Summary

Burundi's physics infrastructure is primarily concentrated at the University of Burundi, which houses:
- The main Department of Physics in the Faculty of Science
- The Research Center in Mathematics and Physics (CRMP) with two research laboratories
- The Doctoral School for advanced studies
- The Institute of Applied Pedagogy for physics teacher training

Private universities provide additional physics education capacity, particularly at the undergraduate level. The country has strong international connections through prominent physicists like Prof. Juma Shabani, who holds leadership positions in regional and international scientific organizations.

Research output from Burundi physicists focuses on theoretical physics (quantum mechanics, mathematical physics), nuclear physics, applied optics, and physics education, with increasing attention to applied areas like solar energy research relevant to national development needs.
