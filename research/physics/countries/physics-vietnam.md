# Physics in Vietnam: A Comprehensive Overview

Vietnam has a rapidly developing physics research ecosystem with strong government support through the Vietnam Academy of Science and Technology (VAST), the National Foundation for Science and Technology Development (NAFOSTED), and major universities. The country has established international collaborations with CERN and other global research institutions while building domestic research capacity in condensed matter physics, nuclear physics, photonics, and space technology.

---

## 1. National Physics Societies

### Vietnam Physical Society (VPS)

The **Vietnam Physical Society** is the premier professional organization representing physicists in Vietnam.

- **Founded**: 1960s (formal organization under VAST)
- **Mission**: To promote physics research, education, and applications in Vietnam; to foster international scientific cooperation
- **Activities**:
  - Organizes national physics conferences and workshops
  - Publishes scientific journals in physics
  - Promotes physics education at all levels
  - Facilitates networking among physicists nationwide
  - Represents Vietnam in international physics organizations including IUPAP (International Union of Pure and Applied Physics)
- **Membership**: Open to physicists, physics educators, and physics students
- **Affiliation**: Operates under the Vietnam Academy of Science and Technology (VAST)
- **Website**: Associated with VAST (http://www.vast.gov.vn/)

### Vietnam Nuclear Society

- **Focus**: Promoting nuclear science and technology applications
- **Activities**: Conferences, workshops on nuclear physics, radiation safety, and peaceful applications of nuclear technology
- **Collaboration**: Works closely with the Vietnam Institute of Nuclear Science and Technology (VINST)

## 3. National Research Centers and Laboratories

### Institute of Physics (IOP), Vietnam Academy of Science and Technology

**Founded**: 1975 (established with VAST)

**Location**: 10 Dao Tan, Ba Dinh, Hanoi

**Status**: Leading research institute under VAST

**Overview**: The Institute of Physics is among the top research institutes in VAST for publications in international ISI journals, averaging about 50 papers per year. It has achieved results published in top journals including Nature, Nature Communications, and Physical Review Letters.

**Research Areas**:
1. **Theoretical Physics**: Elementary particle theory, cosmology, condensed matter theory
2. **Computational Physics**: Quantum information, complex systems
3. **Nuclear Physics**: Nuclear structure and reactions
4. **Laser Physics, Optics and Photonics**: Ultrashort pulse lasers, photonic devices
5. **Environmental Physics**: Atmospheric monitoring, pollution analysis
6. **Astrophysics**: High-energy astrophysics, cosmic rays
7. **Biophysics**: Medical applications, biological materials

**Major Facilities**:
- LIDAR systems for aerosol and atmospheric research
- Confocal laser scanning microscope systems
- Ultra-short pulse laser systems
- Cold plasma device for wound treatment (licensed for use in 300+ hospitals)
- Max-DOAS gas pollution analysis systems
- High-resolution dust counting and analysis systems
- Nanomaterials and graphene production facilities

**Notable Scientists**:
- **Prof. Tran Duc Thiep** - Vietnamese Talents Prize 2011
- **Prof. Nguyen Van Do** - Vietnamese Talents Prize 2011
- **Assoc. Prof. Nguyen Ba An** - Ta Quang Buu Prize 2015
- **Dr. Phung Van Dong** - Ta Quang Buu Young Prize 2016

**Website**: https://iop.vast.gov.vn/

---

### Vietnam Institute of Nuclear Science and Technology (VINST)

**Founded**: 1976 (as Institute of Nuclear Physics; reorganized as VINST)

**Location**: 179 Hoang Quoc Viet, Cau Giay, Hanoi

**Status**: Institute under VAST

**Overview**: VINST is Vietnam's primary research institute for nuclear science and technology, focusing on peaceful applications of nuclear technology.

**Research Areas**:
1. **Nuclear Physics**: Structure, reactions, theoretical nuclear physics
2. **Radiation Physics**: Dosimetry, radiation protection
3. **Nuclear Techniques in Industry**: Non-destructive testing, material analysis
4. **Environmental Radioactivity**: Monitoring, isotope hydrology
5. **Nuclear Medicine**: Radioisotope production, imaging techniques

**Major Facilities**:
- **Dalat Nuclear Research Reactor (DNRR)**: 500 kW pool-type research reactor
- Neutron activation analysis laboratory
- Radioisotope production facilities
- Radiation processing facilities
- Gamma irradiation facility

**Applications**:
- Cancer diagnosis and treatment support
- Food preservation through irradiation
- Industrial quality control
- Environmental monitoring

### Vietnam National Space Center (VNSC)

**Founded**: September 16, 2011 (renamed from Vietnam National Satellite Center in July 2017)

**Location**: Hoa Lac Hi-tech Park, Thach That, Hanoi

**Status**: Research center under VAST

**Overview**: VNSC is responsible for implementing Vietnam's space and satellite programs, including satellite development, ground station operations, and space science research.

**Major Projects**:
- **VNSC-1 (MicroDragon)**: Vietnam's first indigenous Earth observation microsatellite (launched 2019)
- **LOTUSat-1**: Synthetic Aperture Radar (SAR) satellite (under development)
- **Ground Station Network**: Satellite tracking and data reception facilities

**Research Areas**:
- Satellite engineering and technology
- Remote sensing and Earth observation
- Space science
- Satellite navigation applications

**Website**: https://vnsc.org.vn/

## 4. Notable Physics Research Areas

### Condensed Matter Physics and Materials Science

Vietnam has strong research programs in:
- **2D Materials**: Graphene, transition metal dichalcogenides (TMDs)
- **Topological Materials**: Topological insulators research
- **Superconductors**: High-temperature superconductivity studies
- **Semiconductor Physics**: For electronics and optoelectronics applications
- **Nanomaterials**: Synthesis and applications in energy, environment, and medicine

### Nuclear Physics and Nuclear Technology

- **Nuclear Structure**: Theoretical and experimental studies at VINST
- **Neutron Physics**: Applications using the Dalat research reactor
- **Radiation Technology**: Industrial and medical applications
- **Nuclear Safety**: Research supporting Vietnam's nuclear power program

### Optics, Photonics, and Laser Physics

- **Laser Development**: Ultrashort pulse lasers at Institute of Physics
- **Photonic Devices**: For communications and sensing
- **Biomedical Optics**: Applications in medical diagnostics and treatment
- **Remote Sensing**: LIDAR systems for environmental monitoring

### Space Science and Technology

- **Satellite Engineering**: Microsatellite development at VNSC
- **Remote Sensing**: Earth observation and data analysis
- **Space Physics**: Ionospheric studies, space weather
- **Space Engineering**: First undergraduate program at IU-VNUHCM

### High-Energy Physics and Cosmology

- **CERN Collaboration**: Vietnam is an associate member of CERN; Vietnamese physicists participate in CMS experiment
- **Theoretical Particle Physics**: Research at VNU-HUS and IOP
- **Cosmology**: Early universe, dark matter, dark energy research
- **Astroparticle Physics**: High-energy cosmic ray studies

### Computational Physics

- **Quantum Information**: Quantum computing and quantum cryptography research
- **Complex Systems**: Statistical physics, network science
- **Materials Simulation**: First-principles calculations, molecular dynamics

### Environmental Physics

- **Atmospheric Physics**: Aerosol monitoring, pollution studies
- **Climate Research**: Using remote sensing and modeling
- **Environmental Monitoring**: Development of monitoring systems
