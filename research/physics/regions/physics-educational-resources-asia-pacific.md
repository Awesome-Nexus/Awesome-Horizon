## 3. Physics Societies

### Japan

#### Physical Society of Japan (JPS)
- **URL**: https://www.jps.or.jp/english/
- **Country/Region**: Japan
- **Description**: Founded in 1877 as Tokyo Mathematical Society, JPS is one of the oldest scientific societies in Japan with approximately 15,000 members. Publishes Journal of the Physical Society of Japan (JPSJ) and Progress of Theoretical and Experimental Physics (PTEP).
- **Type**: Professional Society
- **Access Level**: Membership (Publications access), Free (Selected resources)

#### Japan Society of Applied Physics (JSAP)
- **URL**: https://www.jsap.or.jp/english/
- **Country/Region**: Japan
- **Description**: Professional society for applied physics in Japan with over 22,000 members. Publishes Japanese Journal of Applied Physics and Applied Physics Express.
- **Type**: Professional Society
- **Access Level**: Membership (Full access), Free (Selected resources)

### China

#### Chinese Physical Society (CPS)
- **URL**: http://www.cps-net.org.cn/English.htm
- **Country/Region**: China
- **Description**: Founded in 1932, CPS has approximately 40,000 members and is composed of 31 provincial societies and 28 sub-societies. Member of IUPAP and AAPPS. Publishes multiple journals including Chinese Physics B, Chinese Physics C, and Chinese Physics Letters.
- **Type**: Professional Society
- **Access Level**: Membership (Full access), Free (Selected resources)

### South Korea

#### Korean Physical Society (KPS)
- **URL**: https://www.kps.or.kr/eng/
- **Country/Region**: South Korea
- **Description**: Founded in 1952 during the Korean War, KPS has grown to approximately 15,000 members. Publishes Journal of the Korean Physical Society, Current Applied Physics, and New Physics: Sae Mulli. Member of IUPAP and AAPPS.
- **Type**: Professional Society
- **Access Level**: Membership (Full access), Free (Selected resources)

### India

#### Indian Physics Association (IPA)
- **URL**: http://www.ipa-inindia.org/
- **Country/Region**: India
- **Description**: National physics society of India promoting physics research and education across the country. Organizes conferences and publishes Bulletin of the Indian Physics Association.
- **Type**: Professional Society
- **Access Level**: Membership (Full access), Free (Selected resources)

#### Indian Association of Physics Teachers (IAPT)
- **URL**: http://www.iapt.org.in/
- **Country/Region**: India
- **Description**: Professional organization dedicated to physics education at school and college levels in India. Organizes physics olympiads and teacher training programs.
- **Type**: Professional Society
- **Access Level**: Membership (Full access), Free (Selected educational resources)

### Taiwan

#### Physical Society of Taiwan (TPS)
- **URL**: https://ps-taiwan.org/cpba/
- **Country/Region**: Taiwan
- **Description**: Professional physics society in Taiwan with approximately 2,000+ members. Organizes annual meetings and promotes physics research and education.
- **Type**: Professional Society
- **Access Level**: Membership (Full access), Free (Selected resources)

### Australia

#### Australian Institute of Physics (AIP)
- **URL**: https://www.aip.org.au/
- **Country/Region**: Australia
- **Description**: Professional body representing physicists in Australia. Publishes Australian Physics magazine and organizes physics congress and specialist conferences.

- **URL**: https://www.ips.org.sg/
- **Country/Region**: Singapore
- **Description**: Professional society promoting physics research and education in Singapore. Organizes conferences and educational outreach programs.
