# Physics Educational Resources and Research Institutions in Southeast Asia

## Executive Summary

Southeast Asia has a rapidly growing physics research ecosystem with strong collaborations in high-energy physics (particularly with CERN), astronomy, quantum technology, and materials science. This comprehensive guide covers major physics departments, research centers, CERN collaborations, and regional organizations across Thailand, Malaysia, Indonesia, Philippines, Vietnam, and Singapore.

---

## 1. Major Physics Departments by Country

### 1.1 Thailand

#### **Chulalongkorn University (CU)**
- **Department of Physics**, Faculty of Science

## 3. Physics Research Centers and Facilities

### 3.1 Thailand

#### **National Astronomical Research Institute of Thailand (NARIT)**
- **Type**: Public Organization
- **Established**: 2004 (as NARI), 2008 (as Public Organization)
- **Headquarters**: Chiang Mai, Thailand
- **Parent**: Ministry of Higher Education, Science, Research and Innovation

**Major Facilities**:
1. **Thai National Observatory (TNO)** - Doi Inthanon (2,450m elevation)
   - 2.4-meter reflecting telescope (one of largest in SE Asia)
   - Located at equator - can observe both northern and southern celestial hemispheres

2. **Thai National Radio Observatory (TNRO)** - Doi Saket District
   - 40-meter radio telescope (largest in Southeast Asia)
   - First radio telescope on the equator
   - 13-meter telescope for VLBI geodesy

3. **Princess Sirindhorn AstroPark** (Chiang Mai)
   - 86,400 sq meters
   - Full-dome digital planetarium (8K, 17m diameter, 160 seats)
   - Optics Laboratory, Radio Astronomy Center

4. **Regional Observatories for the Public**:
   - Nakhon Ratchasima (opened 2014) - at Suranaree University of Technology
   - Chachoengsao (opened 2018)
   - Songkhla (opened 2019)
   - Khon Kaen (under construction)
   - Phitsanulok (under construction)

5. **Thai Robotic Telescope Network (TRT)**:
   - Chile (CTIO)
   - China (Gao Mei Gu Observatory)
   - USA (Sierra Remote Observatories)
   - Australia (Springbrook Observatory)

- **URL**: https://www.narit.or.th/

#### **National Science and Technology Development Agency (NSTDA)**
- **Established**: 30 December 1991
- **Location**: Thailand Science Park, Pathum Thani
- **Mission**: Research, development, technology transfer, human resources
- **Affiliated Centers**:
  - National Electronics and Computer Technology Center (NECTEC)
  - National Metal and Materials Technology Center (MTEC)
  - National Nanotechnology Center (NANOTEC)
  - Thailand Institute of Scientific and Technological Research (TISTR)
- **URL**: https://www.nstda.or.th/

#### **Synchrotron Light Research Institute (SLRI)**
- **Location**: Nakhon Ratchasima
- **CERN Connection**: ALICE Associate Member
- **Facility**: Siam Photon Laboratory
