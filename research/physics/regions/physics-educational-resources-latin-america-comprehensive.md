## 1. Top Universities with Physics Programs

### Brazil

#### 1.1 Instituto de Física da Universidade de São Paulo (IFUSP)
- **URL:** https://portal.if.usp.br/
- **Country:** Brazil
- **Description:** The largest and oldest physics research and teaching institution in Brazil. Offers undergraduate (bachelor's and teaching degree) and graduate (master's and PhD) programs. Home to 118 faculty members and over 1,100 undergraduate students. Research areas include nuclear physics, condensed matter, optics, biophysics, atomic and molecular physics, cosmology, and particle physics.
- **Type:** University Physics Institute
- **Access Level:** Public/Free (Undergraduate), Scholarships available (Graduate)

#### 1.2 Instituto de Física de São Carlos - USP (IFSC)
- **URL:** https://www.ifsc.usp.br/
- **Country:** Brazil
- **Description:** One of Brazil's largest physics institutions with 85 scientists and 250 PhD students. The graduate program has consistently received the maximum evaluation mark by CAPES. Offers multidisciplinary research from theoretical physics to applied physics.
- **Type:** University Physics Institute
- **Access Level:** Public/Free

#### 1.3 Instituto de Física da UFRGS
- **URL:** https://www.if.ufrgs.br/if/
- **Country:** Brazil
- **Description:** Physics Institute at the Federal University of Rio Grande do Sul. Offers bachelor's, teaching degree, and engineering physics programs. Founded in 1959, it includes the Department of Astronomy and operates the university's Astronomical Observatory (founded 1908). Research areas include theoretical physics, experimental physics, and astronomy.
- **Type:** University Physics Institute
- **Access Level:** Public/Free

#### 1.4 Instituto de Física - UNICAMP
- **URL:** https://portal.ifi.unicamp.br/
- **Country:** Brazil
- **Description:** Physics Institute at the State University of Campinas, one of Brazil's premier research institutions. Strong emphasis on condensed matter physics, statistical physics, and interdisciplinary research.
- **Type:** University Physics Institute
- **Access Level:** Public/Free

### Mexico

#### 1.5 Universidad Nacional Autónoma de México (UNAM) - Facultad de Ciencias
- **URL:** https://www.fciencias.unam.mx/
- **Country:** Mexico
- **Description:** Mexico's largest and most prestigious university. The Faculty of Sciences offers comprehensive physics programs at undergraduate and graduate levels. Home to the Mexican Physical Society and various research institutes.
- **Type:** University Faculty
- **Access Level:** Public/Free

#### 1.6 Centro de Investigaciones en Óptica (CIO)
- **URL:** https://www.cio.mx/
- **Country:** Mexico
- **Description:** Research center specializing in optics and photonics. Offers graduate programs (MSc and PhD in Optics, MSc in Optomechatronics). Founded in 1980, it is Mexico's first research institution dedicated exclusively to optics. Features a Science Museum and planetarium.
- **Type:** Research Center / Graduate Institution
- **Access Level:** Graduate programs with scholarships

### Argentina

#### 1.7 Universidad de Buenos Aires (UBA) - Facultad de Ciencias Exactas y Naturales (FCEN)
- **URL:** https://exactas.uba.ar/
- **Country:** Argentina
- **Description:** Argentina's most prestigious public university. The Faculty of Exact and Natural Sciences offers comprehensive physics degrees. Known for excellence in theoretical physics and strong research output.
- **Type:** University Faculty
- **Access Level:** Public/Free

#### 1.8 Instituto Balseiro
- **URL:** https://www.ib.edu.ar/
- **Country:** Argentina
- **Description:** Prestigious physics and engineering institute located in Bariloche. Associated with the National Atomic Energy Commission (CNEA) and the National University of Cuyo. Offers undergraduate and graduate programs with strong emphasis on nuclear physics and condensed matter.
- **Type:** University Institute
- **Access Level:** Public with competitive admission

#### 1.9 Instituto de Física La Plata (IFLP)
- **URL:** http://www.iflp.unlp.edu.ar/
- **Country:** Argentina
- **Description:** Research institute associated with CONICET and the National University of La Plata. Active in theoretical and experimental physics research.
- **Type:** Research Institute
- **Access Level:** Graduate programs

### Chile

#### 1.10 Pontificia Universidad Católica de Chile (UC) - Instituto de Física
- **URL:** https://fisica.uc.cl/
- **Country:** Chile
- **Description:** Top-ranked Chilean university with excellent physics programs. Offers Bachelor's, Master's (Physics and Medical Physics), and PhD programs. Research areas include high energy physics, nonlinear mathematical physics, medical physics, plasma physics, condensed matter, and quantum optics. Recently awarded project to build Chile's first universal quantum computer.
- **Type:** University Institute
- **Access Level:** Private university with scholarships

#### 1.11 Universidad de Chile - Facultad de Ciencias Físicas y Matemáticas (FCFM)
- **URL:** https://ingenieria.uchile.cl/
- **Country:** Chile
- **Description:** Chile's oldest public university with strong physics tradition. The Department of Physics offers Bachelor, Master, and PhD programs. Houses specialized research laboratories including Surface Physics, Crystallography, Micromechanics, and Matter Out of Balance labs.
- **Type:** University Faculty
- **Access Level:** Public/Free

### Colombia

#### 1.12 Universidad de los Andes - Departamento de Física
- **URL:** https://fisica.uniandes.edu.co/
- **Country:** Colombia
- **Description:** Top private university in Colombia with strong physics research. Offers undergraduate and graduate physics programs with emphasis on condensed matter, biophysics, and high-energy physics.
- **Type:** University Department
- **Access Level:** Private with scholarships

#### 1.13 Universidad Nacional de Colombia - Departamento de Física
- **URL:** https://www.fisica.unal.edu.co/
- **Country:** Colombia
- **Description:** Colombia's largest public university system with physics departments at multiple campuses (Bogotá, Medellín, Manizales). Comprehensive undergraduate and graduate programs.
- **Type:** University Department
- **Access Level:** Public/Free

### Uruguay

#### 1.14 Universidad de la República - Facultad de Ciencias - Instituto de Física
- **URL:** https://www.fcien.edu.uy/fisica
- **Country:** Uruguay
- **Description:** Uruguay's only public university. The Institute of Physics offers degrees in Physics, Astronomy, Atmospheric Sciences, and Medical Physics. Features 46 faculty members and strong research in ultrasonics, astronomy, atmospheric science, high-energy physics, statistical mechanics, and quantum field theory.
- **Type:** University Institute
- **Access Level:** Public/Free

### Peru

#### 1.15 Universidad Nacional Mayor de San Marcos (UNMSM) - Facultad de Ciencias Físicas
- **URL:** https://www.fcf.unmsm.edu.pe/
- **Country:** Peru
- **Description:** Peru's oldest university (founded 1551). The Faculty of Physical Sciences offers comprehensive physics programs and hosts important physics conferences including the Peruvian Physics Symposium.
- **Type:** University Faculty
- **Access Level:** Public/Free

## 2. Physics Societies

#### 2.1 Sociedad Brasileira de Física (SBF)
- **URL:** https://www.sbfisica.org.br/
- **Country:** Brazil
- **Description:** Brazilian Physical Society, founded in 1946. Promotes physics research and education, organizes conferences, and publishes scientific journals. Coordinates the Brazilian Physics Olympiad (OBF).
- **Type:** Professional Society
- **Access Level:** Membership-based

#### 2.2 Sociedad Mexicana de Física (SMF)
- **URL:** https://smf.mx/
- **Country:** Mexico
- **Description:** Mexican Physical Society, founded in 1950. Organizes the National Congress of Physics and the National Physics Olympiad. Publishes Revista Mexicana de Física. Accredits physics education programs through CAPEF (Council for Educational Programs in Physics).
- **Type:** Professional Society
- **Access Level:** Membership-based

#### 2.3 Asociación Física Argentina (AFA)
- **URL:** http://www.fisica.org.ar/
- **Country:** Argentina
- **Description:** Argentine Physics Association. Promotes physics research and education throughout Argentina.
- **Type:** Professional Society
- **Access Level:** Membership-based

#### 2.4 Sociedad Chilena de Física (SOCHIFI)
- **URL:** http://www.sochifi.cl/
- **Country:** Chile
- **Description:** Chilean Physical Society, founded in 1965. Organizes the Chilean Physics Symposium every two years and the Chilean Physics Olympiad. Member of IUPAP, FEIASOFI, and FELASOFI.
- **Type:** Professional Society
- **Access Level:** Membership-based

#### 2.5 Sociedad Colombiana de Física (SCF)
- **URL:** http://www.scf.gov.co/
- **Country:** Colombia
- **Description:** Colombian Physical Society. Promotes physics development through conferences, workshops, and educational initiatives.
- **Type:** Professional Society
- **Access Level:** Membership-based

#### 2.6 Sociedad Peruana de Física (SOPERFI)
- **URL:** https://soperfi.org/
- **Country:** Peru
- **Description:** Peruvian Physical Society, founded in 1975. Organizes the Peruvian Physics Symposium (SPF), the most important physics event in Peru. Promotes physics research and teaching improvement at all levels.
- **Type:** Professional Society
- **Access Level:** Membership-based

#### 2.7 Federación Iberoamericana de Sociedades de Física (FEIASOFI)
- **URL:** https://feiasofi.net/
- **Country:** International (Ibero-America)
- **Description:** Ibero-American Federation of Physics Societies, coordinating physics societies across Latin America, Spain, and Portugal. Promotes the Ibero-American Physics Olympiad (OIbF).
- **Type:** International Federation
- **Access Level:** Society membership

## 3. Research Institutions and Laboratories

#### 3.1 Centro Brasileiro de Pesquisas Físicas (CBPF)
- **URL:** https://www.gov.br/cbpf/pt-br
- **Country:** Brazil
- **Description:** Brazilian Center for Physics Research, founded in 1949. One of Brazil's most important research institutions, founded by César Lattes and other prominent physicists. Features 22+ laboratories in theoretical and experimental physics, cosmology, condensed matter, and high-energy physics. Hosts the CBPF School with courses for undergraduate and graduate students.
- **Type:** National Research Institute
- **Access Level:** Research programs and visiting programs

#### 3.2 Instituto Nacional de Pesquisas Espaciais (INPE)
- **URL:** https://www.gov.br/inpe/pt-br
- **Country:** Brazil
- **Description:** National Institute for Space Research, founded in 1961. Brazil's main civilian space research center. Offers graduate programs in astrophysics, earth system science, space geophysics, and remote sensing. Features the Integration and Testing Laboratory (LIT) for satellites.
- **Type:** National Research Institute
- **Access Level:** Graduate programs and research collaborations

#### 3.3 Centro Latinoamericano de Física (CLAF)
- **URL:** http://www.claf.info/
- **Country:** Brazil (Regional)
- **Description:** Latin American Center for Physics, founded in 1962. Promotes physics research and education throughout Latin America. Organizes the Latin American School of Physics and coordinates regional collaborations.
- **Type:** Regional Research Organization
- **Access Level:** Research programs and schools

#### 3.4 Laboratório Nacional de Luz Síncrotron (LNLS/CNPEM)
- **URL:** https://www.lnls.cnpem.br/
- **Country:** Brazil
- **Description:** National Synchrotron Light Laboratory, part of the National Center for Research in Energy and Materials. The only synchrotron facility in Latin America, enabling advanced research in physics, chemistry, materials science, and biology.
- **Type:** National Laboratory
- **Access Level:** Research proposals and beamtime applications

#### 3.5 Instituto Nacional de Astrofísica, Óptica y Electrónica (INAOE)
- **URL:** https://www.inaoep.mx/
- **Country:** Mexico
- **Description:** National Institute of Astrophysics, Optics and Electronics. Offers graduate programs in astrophysics, optics, and electronics. Houses the Large Millimeter Telescope Alfonso Serrano (LMT/GTM).
- **Type:** National Research Institute
- **Access Level:** Graduate programs

#### 3.6 Centro de Investigaciones en Óptica (CIO)
- **URL:** https://www.cio.mx/
- **Country:** Mexico
- **Description:** Center for Research in Optics, founded in 1980. Mexico's first optics research center, located in León, Guanajuato. Offers MSc and PhD programs in optics. Features a Science Museum and public outreach programs.
- **Type:** Research Center
- **Access Level:** Graduate programs

## 5. Physics Olympiads and Competitions

#### 5.1 Olimpiada Iberoamericana de Física (OIbF)
- **URL:** https://www.oibf.org/
- **Country:** International (Ibero-America)
- **Description:** Ibero-American Physics Olympiad, founded in 1991. Annual competition for high school students from 22 Ibero-American countries. Features theoretical and experimental exams. Organized by FEIASOFI and supported by physics societies across the region.
- **Type:** International Competition
- **Access Level:** National team selection

#### 5.2 Olimpíada Brasileira de Física (OBF)
- **URL:** https://www.sbfisica.org.br/olimpiada/
- **Country:** Brazil
- **Description:** Brazilian Physics Olympiad, organized by the Brazilian Physical Society. Three levels covering middle school, high school, and university entrance preparation. Selects students for international competitions.
- **Type:** National Competition
- **Access Level:** Open to students

#### 5.3 Olimpiada Mexicana de Física
- **URL:** https://smf.mx/programas/olimpiada-nacional-de-fisica/
- **Country:** Mexico
- **Description:** Mexican Physics Olympiad, organized by the Mexican Physical Society. Selects students to represent Mexico at the International Physics Olympiad (IPhO) and the Ibero-American Physics Olympiad (OIbF).
- **Type:** National Competition
- **Access Level:** Open to students

#### 5.4 Olimpiada Argentina de Física
- **URL:** http://oaf.df.uba.ar/
- **Country:** Argentina
- **Description:** Argentine Physics Olympiad. Selects students for international physics competitions including IPhO and OIbF.
- **Type:** National Competition
- **Access Level:** Open to students

#### 5.5 Olimpiada Chilena de Física
- **URL:** http://olimpiadafisica.uchile.cl/
- **Country:** Chile
- **Description:** Chilean Physics Olympiad, organized by SOCHIFI. Held annually since 1986. Selects students for the OIbF and IPhO.
- **Type:** National Competition
- **Access Level:** Open to students

#### 5.6 Olimpiada Colombiana de Física
- **URL:** https://olimpiadadefisica.com/
- **Country:** Colombia
- **Description:** Colombian Physics Olympiad. Organizes regional and national competitions to identify talented physics students.
- **Type:** National Competition
- **Access Level:** Open to students

#### 5.7 Olimpiada Peruana de Física
- **URL:** https://www.facebook.com/OlimpiadaPeruanadeFisica/
- **Country:** Peru
- **Description:** Peruvian Physics Olympiad, organized by SOPERFI. Selects students for the Ibero-American Physics Olympiad.
- **Type:** National Competition
- **Access Level:** Open to students
