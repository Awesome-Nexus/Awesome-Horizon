## 1. INTERNATIONAL PHYSICS ORGANIZATIONS

### 1.1 International Union of Pure and Applied Physics (IUPAP)
- **URL:** https://www.iupap.org/
- **Region:** International (Global)
- **Founded:** 1922
- **Members:** 52 territorial members (national physics communities)
- **Description:** The global federation of national physics societies and the supreme international authority in physics. IUPAP promotes international cooperation in physics, sponsors international conferences, and fosters international agreements on symbols, units, nomenclature, and standards. Organizes International Year of Basic Sciences for Sustainable Development.
- **Type:** International Union/Federation
- **Membership Info:** Membership through national adhering bodies (territorial members)

### 1.2 European Physical Society (EPS)
- **URL:** https://www.eps.org/
- **Region:** Europe
- **Founded:** 1968
- **Members:** 42 national societies, representing 130,000+ physicists
- **Description:** Federation of European national physics societies. Promotes physics and physicists in Europe through conferences, publications (Europhysics Letters, EPL), and policy advocacy. Organizes the European Physics Congress and awards the Edison Volta Prize, Lise Meitner Prize, and others.
- **Type:** Regional Federation
- **Membership Info:** Individual membership through national member societies

### 1.3 Association of Asia Pacific Physical Societies (AAPPS)
- **URL:** https://www.aapps.org/
- **Region:** Asia-Pacific
- **Founded:** 1989
- **Members:** 21 member societies across Asia-Pacific region
- **Description:** Promotes physics collaboration in the Asia-Pacific region. Organizes the Asia Pacific Physics Conference (APPC) and represents the region in international physics forums. Member societies include Australia, China, India, Japan, Korea, and others.
- **Type:** Regional Federation
- **Membership Info:** Through member national societies

### 1.4 American Institute of Physics (AIP)
- **URL:** https://www.aip.org/
- **Region:** USA (with international reach)
- **Founded:** 1931
- **Members:** 10 member societies
- **Description:** Federation of American physics societies that advances, promotes, and serves the physical sciences. Publishes Physics Today, maintains the Center for History of Physics, and advocates for physics research funding. Member societies include APS, AAPT, AAS, and others.
- **Type:** Professional Federation
- **Membership Info:** Through member societies or individual programs

### 1.5 African Physical Society (AfPS)
- **URL:** https://www.africanphysicalsociety.org/
- **Region:** Africa (Pan-African)
- **Founded:** 1983 (as SAPAM), relaunched 2010
- **Members:** National physics societies from across Africa
- **Description:** Pan-African professional association for physicists. Enhances physics education, research, and training in Africa. Has observer status with the African Union and promotes regional physics collaboration.
- **Type:** Regional Federation
- **Membership Info:** Open to African national physics societies and individual physicists

### 1.6 International Centre for Theoretical Physics (ICTP)
- **URL:** https://www.ictp.it/
- **Region:** International (based in Trieste, Italy)
- **Founded:** 1964
- **Description:** UNESCO and IAEA-affiliated research institute promoting theoretical physics in developing countries. Founded by Nobel Laureate Abdus Salam. Offers training programs, conferences, and research opportunities for scientists from developing nations.
- **Type:** International Research Institution
- **Membership Info:** Visiting researcher programs, training courses, associate membership schemes

### 1.7 International Atomic Energy Agency (IAEA) - Physics Section
- **URL:** https://www.iaea.org/
- **Region:** International (UN Agency)
- **Founded:** 1957
- **Description:** UN agency promoting peaceful use of nuclear technology. Supports physics research through technical cooperation programs, research contracts, and scientific forums. Co-sponsors ICTP and supports nuclear physics education globally.
- **Type:** International Agency
- **Membership Info:** 175 member states

### 1.8 Abdus Salam International Centre for Theoretical Physics (AS-ICTP)
- **URL:** https://www.ictp.it/
- **Region:** International (Trieste, Italy)
- **Founded:** 1964
- **Description:** Premier international research institute for theoretical physics, operated under UNESCO and IAEA auspices. Mission to foster advanced studies and research in physical and mathematical sciences, especially in developing countries.
- **Type:** International Research Institution
- **Membership Info:** Research associateships, diploma programs, training courses

### 1.9 Asia Pacific Center for Theoretical Physics (APCTP)
- **URL:** https://www.apctp.org/
- **Region:** Asia-Pacific (Pohang, South Korea)
- **Founded:** 1996
- **Members:** 17 member countries/regions
- **Description:** Promotes theoretical physics research and collaboration in the Asia-Pacific region. Organizes workshops, schools, and joint research programs. Member countries include Australia, China, India, Japan, Korea, Taiwan, and others.
- **Type:** Regional Research Organization
- **Membership Info:** Through member country institutions
