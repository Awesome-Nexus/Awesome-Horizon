## 1. Physics Research Databases

### arXiv
- **URL:** https://arxiv.org
- **Description:** A free distribution service and open-access archive for nearly 2.4 million scholarly articles in physics, mathematics, computer science, and related fields. Contains preprints in astrophysics, condensed matter, high energy physics, nuclear physics, quantum physics, and general physics.
- **Type:** Database
- **Access Level:** Free/Open Access

### INSPIRE-HEP
- **URL:** https://inspirehep.net
- **Description:** The leading information platform for high energy physics, providing comprehensive coverage of scientific literature, conferences, institutions, and researchers in particle physics and related fields.
- **Type:** Database
- **Access Level:** Free

### Web of Science
- **URL:** https://www.webofscience.com
- **Description:** A comprehensive research database providing citation indexing and analysis across multiple disciplines, including physics. Tracks research impact and citation networks.
- **Type:** Database
- **Access Level:** Commercial (Institutional subscription required)

### Scopus
- **URL:** https://www.scopus.com
- **Description:** Large abstract and citation database of peer-reviewed literature, including physics journals, conference proceedings, and books. Provides tools for tracking, analyzing, and visualizing research.
- **Type:** Database
- **Access Level:** Commercial (Institutional subscription required)

### NASA ADS (Astrophysics Data System)
- **URL:** https://ui.adsabs.harvard.edu
- **Description:** Digital library portal for researchers in astronomy and physics, operated by the Smithsonian Astrophysical Observatory. Provides abstracts and full-text of major astronomy and physics publications.
- **Type:** Database
- **Access Level:** Free

### Google Scholar
- **URL:** https://scholar.google.com
- **Description:** Free search engine for scholarly literature across various disciplines, including physics. Indexes articles, theses, books, preprints, and technical reports.
- **Type:** Database
- **Access Level:** Free

### PubMed Central
- **URL:** https://www.ncbi.nlm.nih.gov/pmc
- **Description:** Free full-text archive of biomedical and life sciences journal literature, including biophysics and medical physics research.
- **Type:** Database
- **Access Level:** Free

### ScienceDirect
- **URL:** https://www.sciencedirect.com
- **Description:** Platform for peer-reviewed literature operated by Elsevier, covering physics and related scientific fields. Provides access to journal articles and book chapters.
- **Type:** Database
- **Access Level:** Commercial (Mixed open access and subscription content)

### SPIE Digital Library
- **URL:** https://www.spiedigitallibrary.org
- **Description:** Extensive collection of research on optics and photonics, including physics applications in imaging, lasers, and optical engineering.
- **Type:** Database
- **Access Level:** Commercial (Institutional subscription required)

### APS Journals (American Physical Society)
- **URL:** https://journals.aps.org
- **Description:** Premier journals in physics including Physical Review Letters, Physical Review A-X, and Reviews of Modern Physics. High-impact peer-reviewed research publications.
- **Type:** Database
- **Access Level:** Commercial (Some open access content)

## 2. Data Repositories

### Zenodo
- **URL:** https://zenodo.org
- **Description:** Open-access repository operated by CERN, enabling researchers to share and preserve any research outputs including datasets, software, and publications. Supports DOI assignment.
- **Type:** Repository
- **Access Level:** Free/Open Access

### Figshare
- **URL:** https://figshare.com
- **Description:** Online open-access repository where researchers can share research outputs including figures, datasets, images, and videos. Provides DOIs for all content.
- **Type:** Repository
- **Access Level:** Free/Open Access

### Dryad
- **URL:** https://datadryad.org
- **Description:** Curated resource that makes research data discoverable, freely reusable, and citable. Focused on underlying data associated with scientific and medical publications.
- **Type:** Repository
- **Access Level:** Free/Open Access

### Harvard Dataverse
- **URL:** https://dataverse.harvard.edu
- **Description:** Open-source application for publishing, sharing, referencing, extracting, and analyzing research data. Provides persistent identifiers for datasets.
- **Type:** Repository
- **Access Level:** Free/Open Access

### CERN Open Data Portal
- **URL:** https://opendata.cern.ch
- **Description:** Access to open data from CERN experiments, including data from LHC experiments. Provides research-level data for educational and research purposes.
- **Type:** Repository
- **Access Level:** Free/Open Access

### Mendeley Data
- **URL:** https://data.mendeley.com
- **Description:** Research data repository where researchers can upload, share, and get credit for their research data. Provides DOIs for datasets.
- **Type:** Repository
- **Access Level:** Free/Open Access

### Open Science Framework (OSF)
- **URL:** https://osf.io
- **Description:** Free, open-source project management and collaboration tool for researchers, supporting the entire research lifecycle including data storage and sharing.
- **Type:** Repository
- **Access Level:** Free/Open Source

### PubChem
- **URL:** https://pubchem.ncbi.nlm.nih.gov
- **Description:** World's largest collection of freely accessible chemical information, including physical and chemical properties useful for computational physics.
- **Type:** Repository
- **Access Level:** Free

### NIST Physical Measurement Laboratory
- **URL:** https://physics.nist.gov
- **Description:** Authoritative source for physical constants, atomic and molecular spectroscopic data, and other physical reference data maintained by NIST.
- **Type:** Repository
- **Access Level:** Free

### CODATA
- **URL:** https://www.codata.org
- **Description:** Committee on Data of the International Science Council, promoting international cooperation on data and information for science. Maintains recommended values of fundamental physical constants.
- **Type:** Repository/Standards Body
- **Access Level:** Free

## 3. Physics Simulation Software

### PhET Interactive Simulations
- **URL:** https://phet.colorado.edu
- **Description:** Free interactive simulations for teaching and learning physics, chemistry, math, and other sciences. Created by University of Colorado Boulder.
- **Type:** Simulation Software
- **Access Level:** Free/Open Source

### COMSOL Multiphysics
- **URL:** https://www.comsol.com
- **Description:** Comprehensive simulation software environment for modeling and simulating physics-based problems. Supports coupled physics phenomena including electromagnetics, structural mechanics, acoustics, and fluid flow.
- **Type:** Simulation Software
- **Access Level:** Commercial (Free trial available)

### VPython
- **URL:** https://vpython.org
- **Description:** 3D programming environment for Python that makes it easy to create navigable 3D displays and animations, particularly useful for physics education and visualization.
- **Type:** Simulation Software
- **Access Level:** Free/Open Source

### OpenFOAM
- **URL:** https://www.openfoam.com
- **Description:** Free, open-source CFD (Computational Fluid Dynamics) software developed for solving complex fluid flows, turbulence, heat transfer, and multiphysics problems.
- **Type:** Simulation Software
- **Access Level:** Free/Open Source

### FEniCS Project
- **URL:** https://fenicsproject.org
- **Description:** Popular open-source computing platform for solving partial differential equations (PDEs) using the finite element method. High-level Python and C++ interfaces.
- **Type:** Simulation Software
- **Access Level:** Free/Open Source

### Firedrake
- **URL:** https://firedrakeproject.org
- **Description:** Automated system for solving PDEs using the finite element method. Uses sophisticated code generation for high-performance simulations.
- **Type:** Simulation Software
- **Access Level:** Free/Open Source

### LAMMPS
- **URL:** https://www.lammps.org
- **Description:** Large-scale Atomic/Molecular Massively Parallel Simulator - classical molecular dynamics simulation code designed for materials modeling and soft matter physics.
- **Type:** Simulation Software
- **Access Level:** Free/Open Source

### GROMACS
- **URL:** https://www.gromacs.org
- **Description:** Versatile package for molecular dynamics simulations, particularly designed for biomolecular systems but applicable to physics simulations.
- **Type:** Simulation Software
- **Access Level:** Free/Open Source

### Quantum ESPRESSO
- **URL:** https://www.quantum-espresso.org
- **Description:** Integrated suite of open-source computer codes for electronic-structure calculations and materials modeling at the nanoscale using density functional theory.
- **Type:** Simulation Software
- **Access Level:** Free/Open Source

### CP2K
- **URL:** https://www.cp2k.org
- **Description:** Quantum chemistry and solid-state physics software package for atomistic simulations of solid, liquid, molecular, and biological systems.
- **Type:** Simulation Software
- **Access Level:** Free/Open Source

## 4. Computational Physics Tools

### Python (with Scientific Stack)
- **URL:** https://www.python.org
- **Description:** High-level programming language with extensive libraries for scientific computing. The foundation for modern computational physics.
- **Type:** Programming Language/Tool
- **Access Level:** Free/Open Source

### NumPy
- **URL:** https://numpy.org
- **Description:** Fundamental package for scientific computing with Python. Provides support for large, multi-dimensional arrays and matrices, along with mathematical functions.
- **Type:** Library
- **Access Level:** Free/Open Source

### SciPy
- **URL:** https://scipy.org
- **Description:** Library for scientific computing and technical computing. Provides modules for optimization, integration, interpolation, eigenvalue problems, and more.
- **Type:** Library
- **Access Level:** Free/Open Source

### SymPy
- **URL:** https://www.sympy.org
- **Description:** Python library for symbolic mathematics. Provides computer algebra system (CAS) capabilities including algebraic manipulations, calculus, and equation solving.
- **Type:** Library
- **Access Level:** Free/Open Source

### MATLAB
- **URL:** https://www.mathworks.com/products/matlab.html
- **Description:** High-level language and interactive environment for numerical computation, visualization, and programming. Widely used in physics research and education.
- **Type:** Computational Tool
- **Access Level:** Commercial (Student licenses available)

### Mathematica
- **URL:** https://www.wolfram.com/mathematica/
- **Description:** Technical computing system spanning most areas of technical computing, including neural networks, machine learning, image processing, geometry, and data science.
- **Type:** Computational Tool
- **Access Level:** Commercial

### Maple
- **URL:** https://www.maplesoft.com/products/Maple/
- **Description:** Symbolic and numeric computing environment, and multi-paradigm programming language. Covers mathematics, visualization, data analysis, and connectivity.
- **Type:** Computational Tool
- **Access Level:** Commercial

### GNU Octave
- **URL:** https://octave.org
- **Description:** High-level programming language for numerical computations, primarily intended for numerical solutions of linear and nonlinear problems. MATLAB-compatible.
- **Type:** Computational Tool
- **Access Level:** Free/Open Source

### GNU Scientific Library (GSL)
- **URL:** https://www.gnu.org/software/gsl/
- **Description:** Numerical library for C and C++ programmers. Provides over 1000 functions for mathematical routines including random number generators, special functions, and least-squares fitting.
- **Type:** Library
- **Access Level:** Free/Open Source (GPL)

### Jupyter Notebook
- **URL:** https://jupyter.org
- **Description:** Web-based interactive computing platform that allows creating and sharing computational documents. Supports over 40 programming languages.
- **Type:** Computational Tool
- **Access Level:** Free/Open Source

### Pandas
- **URL:** https://pandas.pydata.org
- **Description:** Fast, powerful, flexible data analysis and manipulation tool built on Python. Essential for data processing in physics research.
- **Type:** Library
- **Access Level:** Free/Open Source

### Astropy
- **URL:** https://www.astropy.org
- **Description:** Community-developed core Python package for astronomy. Provides tools and functionality for astronomical calculations, data handling, and analysis.
- **Type:** Library
- **Access Level:** Free/Open Source

### Qiskit
- **URL:** https://qiskit.org
- **Description:** Open-source SDK for quantum computing that enables users to create, manipulate, and run quantum circuits on quantum computers and simulators.
- **Type:** Library/Framework
- **Access Level:** Free/Open Source

## 5. Visualization Tools

### ROOT
- **URL:** https://root.cern
- **Description:** Scientific software framework developed at CERN for data processing, statistical analysis, visualization, and storage. The standard for high-energy physics data analysis.
- **Type:** Visualization/Analysis Tool
- **Access Level:** Free/Open Source

### Matplotlib
- **URL:** https://matplotlib.org
- **Description:** Comprehensive library for creating static, animated, and interactive visualizations in Python. Standard plotting library for scientific computing.
- **Type:** Visualization Library
- **Access Level:** Free/Open Source

### ParaView
- **URL:** https://www.paraview.org
- **Description:** Open-source multiple-platform application for interactive, scientific visualization. Can run on supercomputers to analyze exascale datasets.
- **Type:** Visualization Tool
- **Access Level:** Free/Open Source

### VisIt
- **URL:** https://visit-dav.github.io/visit-website/
- **Description:** Open-source interactive parallel visualization and graphical analysis tool for viewing scientific data. Proven scaling to large datasets.
- **Type:** Visualization Tool
- **Access Level:** Free/Open Source (BSD)

### Mayavi
- **URL:** https://docs.enthought.com/mayavi/mayavi/
- **Description:** Application and library for interactive 3D visualization of scientific data using Python. Built on VTK.
- **Type:** Visualization Library
- **Access Level:** Free/Open Source

### yt
- **URL:** https://yt-project.org
- **Description:** Python package for analyzing and visualizing volumetric data, particularly suited for astrophysical simulations but applicable to various physics domains.
- **Type:** Visualization Library
- **Access Level:** Free/Open Source

### Plotly
- **URL:** https://plotly.com/python/
- **Description:** Interactive graphing library for Python and other languages. Enables creation of interactive, publication-quality graphs online.
- **Type:** Visualization Library
- **Access Level:** Free/Open Source (Commercial features available)

### Seaborn
- **URL:** https://seaborn.pydata.org
- **Description:** Python visualization library based on matplotlib that provides a high-level interface for drawing attractive statistical graphics.
- **Type:** Visualization Library
- **Access Level:** Free/Open Source

### VTK (Visualization Toolkit)
- **URL:** https://vtk.org
- **Description:** Open-source software system for 3D computer graphics, image processing, and visualization. Used in physics research and medical imaging.
- **Type:** Visualization Library
- **Access Level:** Free/Open Source

### Blender
- **URL:** https://www.blender.org
- **Description:** Free and open-source 3D creation suite supporting modeling, animation, simulation, rendering, and video editing. Used for physics visualization and simulation.
- **Type:** Visualization/Animation Tool
- **Access Level:** Free/Open Source

## 6. Open Data Initiatives in Physics

### CERN Open Data
- **URL:** https://opendata.cern.ch
- **Description:** Provides access to open data from CERN particle physics experiments. Includes data from the LHC, making research-level data available for education and research.
- **Type:** Open Data Initiative
- **Access Level:** Free/Open Access

### LIGO Open Science Center
- **URL:** https://www.gw-openscience.org
- **Description:** Provides access to gravitational-wave data from LIGO and Virgo detectors. Enables public access to data from gravitational-wave discoveries.
- **Type:** Open Data Initiative
- **Access Level:** Free/Open Access

### Sloan Digital Sky Survey (SDSS)
- **URL:** https://www.sdss.org
- **Description:** Major multi-filter imaging and spectroscopic redshift survey. Provides public access to astronomical data and has created the most detailed 3D maps of the Universe.
- **Type:** Open Data Initiative
- **Access Level:** Free/Open Access

### Hubble Space Telescope Archive
- **URL:** https://archive.stsci.edu
- **Description:** Public archive of Hubble Space Telescope data. Provides access to observations, images, and spectra from Hubble's instruments.
- **Type:** Open Data Initiative
- **Access Level:** Free/Open Access

### Planck Satellite Data
- **URL:** https://www.cosmos.esa.int/web/planck
- **Description:** Public data releases from the Planck satellite mission, providing precise measurements of the cosmic microwave background radiation.
- **Type:** Open Data Initiative
- **Access Level:** Free/Open Access

### Gaia Archive
- **URL:** https://www.cosmos.esa.int/web/gaia
- **Description:** ESA's mission to chart a three-dimensional map of our Galaxy. Provides precise positions and velocities of stars in the Milky Way.
- **Type:** Open Data Initiative
- **Access Level:** Free/Open Access

### HelioPhysics Science Data
- **URL:** https://heliophysicsdata.gsfc.nasa.gov
- **Description:** NASA's coordinated efforts to make heliophysics data easily available. Covers solar and space physics data.
- **Type:** Open Data Initiative
- **Access Level:** Free/Open Access

### Materials Project
- **URL:** https://materialsproject.org
- **Description:** Open-access database of calculated properties of known and predicted materials. Provides data for materials science and condensed matter physics.
- **Type:** Open Data Initiative
- **Access Level:** Free/Open Access

### PubChem
- **URL:** https://pubchem.ncbi.nlm.nih.gov
- **Description:** World's largest collection of freely accessible chemical information, including molecular properties useful for computational physics.
- **Type:** Open Data Initiative
- **Access Level:** Free/Open Access

### NSF PAR (Public Access Repository)
- **URL:** https://par.nsf.gov
- **Description:** Public access repository for National Science Foundation-funded research, including physics publications and associated data.
- **Type:** Open Data Initiative
- **Access Level:** Free/Open Access
