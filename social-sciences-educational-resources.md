## TIPS FOR LEARNERS

### How to Access These Resources for Free

1. **Coursera**: Select "Audit the course" option (no certificate)
2. **edX**: Select "Audit this course" (access to materials, no certificate)
3. **Open Yale Courses**: Direct download, completely free
4. **MIT OCW**: Direct access, completely free
5. **Marginal Revolution University**: Completely free with optional practice questions

### Building Your Learning Path

1. **Start with fundamentals**: Take introductory courses first
2. **Progress systematically**: Move from introductory to intermediate
3. **Combine theory and application**: Mix lectures with practical exercises
4. **Use multiple resources**: Cross-reference between textbooks and courses
5. **Engage with communities**: Join discussion forums and study groups
