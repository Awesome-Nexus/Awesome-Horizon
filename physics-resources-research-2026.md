#### Current Experiments and Status (2024-2025):
- **LHC Run 3**: Third run began in July 2022, final full year concluded December 2025
- **2025 achievements**: Record-breaking integrated luminosity of 125 fb⁻¹ delivered to ATLAS and CMS
- **Special 2025 runs**: First-ever proton-oxygen, oxygen-oxygen, and neon-neon collisions

#### Key Discoveries and Results (2024-2025):
- ATLAS found evidence for Higgs boson decay to muons (H→μμ) with 3.4σ significance
- CMS reported first measurement of quantum properties of "all-charm" tetraquarks
- Confirmation of quark-gluon plasma formation in oxygen collisions

## 4. Physics Research Frontiers 2025

### James Webb Space Telescope (JWST) Discoveries

| Metric | Value | Source |
|--------|-------|--------|
| Launch date | December 25, 2021 | NASA |
| Operational since | July 2022 | NASA |
| Primary mission | Infrared astronomy | NASA |

#### Major 2024-2025 Discoveries:

##### 1. First Direct-Imaged Exoplanet Discovery (June 2025)
- **Planet**: TWA 7 b
- **Mass**: Saturn-like (10x lighter than previously imaged exoplanets)
- **Distance**: 111 light-years from Earth
- **Significance**: JWST's first direct exoplanet image discovery
- **Method**: Coronagraph on MIRI instrument
- **Published**: Nature, June 25, 2025

##### 2. Alpha Centauri Planet Candidate (August 2025)
- **Location**: Alpha Centauri A (4 light-years away)
- **Type**: Gas giant candidate
- **Orbit**: 1-2 AU from star
- **Significance**: Closest Sun-like star system
- **Status**: Further observations needed for confirmation

##### 3. Unusual Exoplanet Atmosphere (2025)
- **Planet**: PSR J2322-2650b
- **Type**: Planet orbiting pulsar
- **Atmosphere**: Dominated by helium and carbon (C3, C2 molecules)
- **Shape**: Lemon-like due to tidal forces
- **Discovery**: "Black widow" system candidate

## 6. Physics Funding and Scholarships 2026

### NSF Graduate Research Fellowship Program (GRFP)

| Metric | 2024 | 2025 | Change |
|--------|------|------|--------|
| **Awards** | **2,036** | **1,000** | **-51%** |
| Honorable mentions | ~1,900 | ~3,137 | +65% |
| Total recognized | ~3,700 | ~4,000 | +8% |
| Stipend | $37,000/year | $37,000/year | Unchanged |
| Cost of Education allowance | $16,000/year | $16,000/year | Unchanged |

#### Significant 2025 Changes:

1. **Award reduction**: Awards cut by more than half (from ~2,000 to 1,000)
2. **Eligibility change (2026)**: Second-year Ph.D. students are **no longer eligible**
3. **Priority areas**: Emphasis on AI, quantum information science, computationally intensive research
4. **Timeline issues**: 2026 solicitation delayed to September 2025

#### 2025 Cuts by Discipline:

| Field | Reduction |
|-------|-----------|
| Chemistry | -56% |
| Physics and Astronomy | ~-50% |
| Geosciences | ~-50% |
| Life Sciences | ~-50% |
| Engineering | -45% |
| Materials Research | -36% (smallest cut) |

#### Current NSF GRFP Award Structure:
- 3 years of support over 5-year fellowship period
- $37,000 annual stipend
- $16,000 cost of education allowance
- Portable across institutions
