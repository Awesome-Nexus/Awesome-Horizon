### CERN Membership
All five Scandinavian countries are members of CERN:
- Denmark: Member since 1950s, contributes to ALICE
- Finland: Member since 1991, contributes to CMS, ALICE
- Iceland: Associate member
- Norway: Member, contributes to ATLAS, ALICE
- Sweden: Member, contributes to ATLAS, ALICE, ISOLDE
