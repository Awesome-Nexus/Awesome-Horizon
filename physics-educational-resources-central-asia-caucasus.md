## Kazakhstan

### 1. Al-Farabi Kazakh National University - Faculty of Physics and Technology
- **URL:** https://farabi.university/faculty/2?lang=en
- **Country:** Kazakhstan
- **Type:** University
- **Description:** One of the oldest and most prestigious physics faculties in Central Asia, founded in 1934. The faculty consists of 5 departments: Theoretical and Nuclear Physics, Thermophysics and Technical Physics, Plasma Physics and Nanotechnology, Solid State Physics and New Materials Technology, and Electronics and Astrophysics. Offers 51 educational programs with instruction in Kazakh, Russian, and English.
- **Access Level:** Open admission through competitive entrance examinations

### 2. Nazarbayev University - Physics Department
- **URL:** http://ssh.nu.edu.kz/phys_about
- **Country:** Kazakhstan
- **Description:** Premier research university offering cutting-edge physics education. Faculty conducts research in Laser-based Material Science, Functional Nanomaterials, Micro- and Nanofabrication, Electromagnetics, Ultrafast Photonics, General Relativity, Cosmology, Quantum Field Theory, Atomic and Molecular Physics, and Computational Astrophysics. PhD program focuses on priority areas for Kazakhstan's high-tech industry development.
- **Type:** University
- **Access Level:** Competitive admission

### 3. National School of Physics and Mathematics (FIZMAT)
- **URL:** https://izho.kz/
- **Country:** Kazakhstan
- **Description:** Founded in 1972, this is Kazakhstan's premier specialized secondary school for physics and mathematics. Located in Almaty, it serves as a hub for gifted students and hosts the International Zhautykov Olympiad (IZhO) in Mathematics, Physics, and Computer Science, attracting participants from 15+ countries including all Central Asian nations and Caucasus countries.
- **Type:** Specialized Secondary School
- **Access Level:** Competitive entrance examinations

### 4. Republican Scientific and Practical Center "Daryn"
- **URL:** https://daryn.kz/en/
- **Country:** Kazakhstan
- **Description:** A state institution working with gifted children across Kazakhstan. Organizes national and international olympiads, competitions, and educational programs. Key organizer of the International Zhautykov Olympiad and manages Kazakhstan's participation in international science competitions.
- **Type:** Government Initiative/Research Center
- **Access Level:** Through school selection and olympiad participation

### 5. Nazarbayev Intellectual Schools (NIS) - Physics and Mathematics Track
- **URL:** https://www.nis.edu.kz/en/
- **Country:** Kazakhstan
- **Description:** Network of trilingual (Kazakh, Russian, English) schools established in 2008 offering specialized physics and mathematics programs. Features the NIS Programme, the first trilingual educational program in Kazakhstan created in cooperation with Cambridge. Schools located in Almaty, Astana, Aktau, Aktobe, Atyrau, Kokshetau, Kostanay, Pavlodar, Petropavl, Semei, Shymkent, Taldykorgan, Taraz, Oral, and Kyzylorda.
- **Type:** Network of Specialized Schools
- **Access Level:** Competitive selection through national testing

### 6. Institute of Nuclear Physics (Branch of National Nuclear Center)
- **URL:** https://npg.kz/
- **Country:** Kazakhstan
- **Description:** Research facility focusing on nuclear physics with educational programs for undergraduate and graduate students. Collaborates with Nazarbayev University and international partners for research internships and training programs.
- **Type:** Research Institution
- **Access Level:** University partnership programs

## Uzbekistan

### 7. National University of Uzbekistan named after Mirzo Ulugbek - Faculty of Physics
- **URL:** https://nuu.uz/en/faculty/fizika/
- **Country:** Uzbekistan
- **Description:** The faculty was established in 1959 and is one of Central Asia's leading physics education centers. Offers bachelor's degrees in physics and astronomy, master's degrees in 7 specialties, and doctoral programs. Features 73 professors including 4 academicians, 19 doctors of science, and 39 candidates of sciences. The information resource center holds over 100,000 books.
- **Type:** University
- **Access Level:** National entrance examinations

### 8. Tashkent State Technical University - Physics Department
- **URL:** https://tuit.uz/en/fizika
- **Country:** Uzbekistan
- **Description:** Major technical university offering comprehensive physics education as part of engineering programs. Founded in 1955, the Department of Physics serves all faculties of the university with a focus on applied physics for engineering disciplines.
- **Type:** University
- **Access Level:** National entrance examinations

### 9. Institute of Nuclear Physics, Academy of Sciences of Uzbekistan
- **URL:** https://www.inp.uz
- **Country:** Uzbekistan
- **Description:** Leading research institution in nuclear physics in Central Asia. Founded in 1956, conducts research in nuclear physics, radiation physics, and related fields. Offers research opportunities for students and collaborates with international institutions including JINR (Dubna, Russia).
- **Type:** Research Institution
- **Access Level:** Research partnerships, postgraduate programs

### 10. Institute of Ion-Plasma and Laser Technologies, Academy of Sciences
- **Country:** Uzbekistan
- **Description:** Research institute focused on laser physics, plasma physics, and related technologies. Provides practical training and research opportunities for physics students from National University of Uzbekistan and other institutions.
- **Type:** Research Institution
- **Access Level:** University partnerships

### 11. Institute of Astronomy, Academy of Sciences of Uzbekistan
- **Country:** Uzbekistan
- **Description:** Research institute conducting astronomical and astrophysical research. Founded on the basis of the Tashkent Observatory (1873), one of the oldest observatories in Central Asia. Offers research opportunities for students in astronomy and astrophysics.
- **Type:** Research Institution/Observatory
- **Access Level:** Research programs, public outreach

### 12. Uzbekistan Physics Olympiad - Science Olympiad Center
- **URL:** https://www.olympcenter.uz/
- **Country:** Uzbekistan
- **Description:** National center organizing physics olympiads and competitions for secondary school students. Responsible for selecting Uzbekistan's team for International Physics Olympiad (IPhO) and other international competitions.
- **Type:** Competition/Organization
- **Access Level:** School-level selection

## Kyrgyzstan

### 13. Kyrgyz State University named after I. Arabaev - Faculty of Physics and Mathematics
- **URL:** https://arabaevksu.edu.kg/en/institutes/pedagogika-fakul-teti
- **Country:** Kyrgyzstan
- **Description:** Established in 1952 as part of the Kyrgyz Pedagogical Institute. Prepares teachers specializing in mathematics, physics, and physics with fundamentals of production. The faculty has well-equipped laboratories and classrooms for physics and computational mathematics.
- **Type:** University
- **Access Level:** National entrance examinations

### 14. Kyrgyz State Technical University named after I. Razzakov - Physics Department
- **URL:** https://kstu.kg/en/bokovoe-menju/faculties/translate-to-english-ehnergeticheskii-fakultet/physics
- **Country:** Kyrgyzstan
- **Description:** Founded in 1954, one of the oldest physics departments in Kyrgyzstan. Trains bachelors in all technical areas of KSTU and provides physics education across all faculties. Features 6 educational laboratories and a computer class with virtual laboratory works.
- **Type:** University
- **Access Level:** National entrance examinations

### 15. Physics Hub Kyrgyzstan
- **URL:** https://physics-hub.com/en
- **Country:** Kyrgyzstan
- **Description:** International physics community founded by Nursultan Rayapov, an olympiad winner and member of Kyrgyzstan's physics national team. Official source of information about all stages of the National Physics Olympiad in Kyrgyzstan and selection processes for international olympiads. Provides learning materials, physics forum, and olympiad preparation resources.
- **Type:** Educational Platform/Organization
- **Access Level:** Free online access

### 16. International University of Kyrgyzstan - Faculty of Physics
- **Country:** Kyrgyzstan
- **Description:** Private university offering physics education programs. Faculty includes researchers working in atmospheric physics and related disciplines. Collaborates with international institutions for student and faculty exchanges.
- **Type:** University
- **Access Level:** Entrance examinations

### 17. Issyk-Kul State University - Faculty of Physics, Mathematics and Natural Engineering
- **URL:** http://main.iksu.kg/?page_id=1783
- **Country:** Kyrgyzstan
- **Description:** Located in Karakol, this faculty provides physics education with a focus on regional educational needs. Offers programs in physics and mathematics education for the Issyk-Kul region.
- **Type:** University
- **Access Level:** Regional admission

## Tajikistan

- **URL:** https://tnu.tj/index.php/en/faculty-of-physics/
- **Country:** Tajikistan
- **Description:** Founded in 1948 in Dushanbe, the faculty offers comprehensive physics education programs. The university is the leading higher education institution in Tajikistan, with a dedicated Faculty of Physics offering undergraduate and graduate programs in physics and related disciplines.
- **Type:** University
- **Access Level:** National entrance examinations

### 19. S.U. Umarov Physical-Technical Institute, Academy of Sciences of Tajikistan
- **URL:** https://phti.tj/
- **Country:** Tajikistan
- **Description:** Leading research institute named after S.U. Umarov, conducting research in atmospheric physics, renewable energy (solar and wind), and environmental physics. Organizes international scientific conferences on current issues in physics and engineering. Research focuses on climate change in Central Asia and renewable energy systems.
- **Type:** Research Institution
- **Access Level:** Research programs, conferences

### 20. Tajik Technical University named after M.S. Osimi - Department of Physics
- **URL:** http://ef.ttu.tj/en/pages/142
- **Country:** Tajikistan

## Turkmenistan

### 21. Magtymguly Turkmen State University - Faculty of Physics
- **URL:** https://tdu.edu.tm/?/Faculty%20of%20Physics/&page=19&in=61
- **Country:** Turkmenistan
- **Description:** The Faculty of Physics was established in 1932, making it one of the oldest physics faculties in Central Asia. Contains departments of General Physics, Theoretical and Experimental Physics, and Radio Physics and Electronics. Features 23 special rooms equipped with modern technologies from Europe and Russia, plus an observatory. Prepares specialists as "physicist, teacher" and "radio physicist, teacher."
- **Type:** University
- **Access Level:** National entrance examinations

### 22. Gujurly Bilim Merkezi (Science Center)
- **URL:** https://www.gujurly.edu.tm/
- **Country:** Turkmenistan
- **Description:** Private educational center in Ashgabat providing short-term courses on physics, mathematics, computer skills, and languages. Serves as a training center for physics olympiad participants, with instructors who are team leaders for Turkmenistan's International Physics Olympiad (IPhO) team. Offers Talent Show competitions and Golden Bookmark reading contests.
- **Type:** Science Center/Educational Institution
- **Access Level:** Enrollment-based

## Georgia

### 23. Ilia State University - Institute of Physics
- **URL:** https://iliauni.edu.ge/en/iliauni/AcademicDepartments/sainjinro-fakulteti-270/programebi-310
- **Country:** Georgia
- **Description:** Premier physics research and education center in Georgia, formed in 2006. Offers three-level education (Bachelor's, Master's, PhD) with specializations in Elementary Particle Physics, Theoretical Astrophysics, Cosmology and Gravitation, Condensed Matter Physics, Astronomy-Astrophysics, Physics of Atmosphere and Ionosphere, and Biophysics. Home to the Center for Elementary Particle Physics (CEPP) and Institute of Theoretical Physics.
- **Type:** University
- **Access Level:** Competitive admission

### 24. Ivane Javakhishvili Tbilisi State University - Faculty of Physics
- **Country:** Georgia
- **Description:** One of the oldest universities in the Caucasus region (founded 1918) with a strong physics tradition. The Faculty of Physics offers comprehensive physics education and research programs, ranking among the top physics departments in the South Caucasus.
- **Type:** University
- **Access Level:** National entrance examinations

### 25. Ilia Vekua Sukhumi Institute of Physics and Technology (SIPT)
- **URL:** https://www.sipt.org.ge/en
- **Country:** Georgia
- **Description:** Founded in 1945 on the basis of the German scientific community in Sukhumi. Now located in Tbilisi, the institute conducts research in various fields of physics and technology. Offers research opportunities and collaborations with universities.
- **Type:** Research Institution
- **Access Level:** Research programs

### 26. Experimentorium - Science Museum
- **URL:** https://www.tripadvisor.com/Attraction_Review-g294195-d11876340-Reviews-Experimentorium_Science_Museum-Tbilisi.html
- **Country:** Georgia
- **Description:** Interactive science museum in Tbilisi featuring approximately 80 exhibits that demonstrate laws of physics, biology, mathematics, and anatomy. All exhibits are touchable and interactive, making physics concepts accessible to visitors of all ages. Open daily from 10:00 AM to 7:00 PM.
- **Type:** Science Museum
- **Access Level:** Public (admission fee)

### 27. Georgian National Astrophysical Observatory (Abastumani)
- **Country:** Georgia
- **Description:** Historic observatory founded in 1932, located at an altitude of 1,700 meters. Affiliated with Ilia State University and conducting research in astrophysics and astronomy. Offers educational programs and public outreach activities.
- **Type:** Research Institution/Observatory
- **Access Level:** Research programs, public visits

## Armenia

### 28. Yerevan State University - Institute of Physics
- **URL:** https://www.ysu.am/en/faculty/525
- **Country:** Armenia
- **Description:** Formed in 2022 by merging the Faculty of Physics (founded 1959) and Faculty of Radiophysics (founded 1975). Trains specialists in mathematical physics with a three-level education system (undergraduate, master's, postgraduate). Includes the Research Institute of Physics (founded 2016). Notable alumni include members of the Armenian Academy of Sciences.
- **Type:** University
- **Access Level:** National entrance examinations

### 29. Yerevan Physics Institute (YerPhi)
- **URL:** https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Yerevan_Physics_Institute
- **Country:** Armenia
- **Description:** Founded in 1943 by brothers Academicians Abram Alikhanov and Artem Alikhanian as a branch of Yerevan State University. Major research center for high-energy physics and cosmic ray research. Houses the A. Alikhanian National Laboratory (AANL) with experimental facilities for international collaborations.
- **Type:** Research Institution
- **Access Level:** Research programs, summer schools

### 30. Byurakan Astrophysical Observatory
- **URL:** https://www.bao.am/
- **Country:** Armenia
- **Description:** Founded in 1946 by Academician Viktor Ambartsumian, the observatory is located on the slopes of Mt. Aragats at 1,500m altitude. One of the most famous astronomical observatories in the former Soviet Union. Conducts research in astrophysics with 9 research departments including Astronomical Surveys, Young Stellar Objects, and Astrochemistry. Designated as a National Value of Armenia.
- **Type:** Research Institution/Observatory
- **Access Level:** Research programs, public visits, international collaborations

### 31. Institute for Physical Research (IPR), National Academy of Sciences
- **URL:** https://ipr.sci.am/
- **Country:** Armenia
- **Description:** Public research organization working in laser physics, material science, atomic physics, laser spectroscopy, quantum and nonlinear optics, photonics, quantum information, and nanomaterials. Produces over 100 publications annually and hosts the annual international "Laser Physics" conference. Holds the Basic Chair of Quantum and Optical Electronics at Russian-Armenian University.
- **Type:** Research Institution
- **Access Level:** Research programs, international collaborations

### 32. Institute of Applied Problems of Physics (IAPP)
- **URL:** https://www.iapp.am/
- **Country:** Armenia
- **Description:** Founded in 1980, the institute conducts applied research in various fields of physics. Located in Yerevan, it focuses on solving practical problems through physics research and development.
- **Type:** Research Institution
- **Access Level:** Research programs

### 33. Russian-Armenian University - Physics Programs
- **Country:** Armenia
- **Description:** Intergovernmental university established in 1997 through an agreement between Russia and Armenia. Offers physics education with Russian standards and international collaborations. Hosts the Basic Chair of Quantum and Optical Electronics from the Institute for Physical Research.
- **Type:** University
- **Access Level:** Entrance examinations

## Regional Initiatives

### 37. International Zhautykov Olympiad (IZhO)
- **URL:** https://izho.kz/
- **Countries:** Kazakhstan, Central Asia, Caucasus, Eastern Europe
- **Description:** Annual international olympiad in Mathematics, Physics, and Computer Science held in Almaty, Kazakhstan. Attracts over 700 schoolchildren and 150 leaders from 15+ countries including Kyrgyzstan, Uzbekistan, Turkmenistan, Tajikistan, Russia, Belarus, Armenia, Azerbaijan, Turkey, Georgia, Bulgaria, Romania, Serbia, and Mongolia. Organized by the Ministry of Education of Kazakhstan, Republican Scientific and Practical Centre "Daryn", and National School of Physics and Mathematics.
- **Type:** International Competition
- **Access Level:** Through national team selection

### 38. Eurasian Olympiad in Informatics and Physics
- **Countries:** Central Asian countries
- **Description:** Multi-subject olympiad bringing together participants from Kazakhstan, Kyrgyzstan, Uzbekistan, and Tajikistan. The XVI Eurasian Olympiad in Informatics featured over 230 participants and 77 teams from Central Asia. Promotes STEM education and international collaboration among Central Asian students.
- **Type:** Regional Competition
- **Access Level:** School-level selection

### 39. Tournament of Physics Battles in Central Asia
- **URL:** https://aphb.bc-pf.org/en/
- **Countries:** Central Asia
- **Description:** International physics competition for teams from Central Asian countries. Features multiple stages of participation with registration typically opening in October. Teams compete in solving physics problems in a tournament format.
- **Type:** Regional Competition
- **Access Level:** Team registration

### 40. Central Asian University Network for Physics Education
- **Countries:** Kazakhstan, Uzbekistan, Kyrgyzstan, Tajikistan, Turkmenistan
- **Description:** Informal network of universities and research institutions facilitating student exchanges, joint research projects, and curriculum development in physics education across Central Asia. Includes partnerships between Al-Farabi Kazakh National University, National University of Uzbekistan, Kyrgyz State University, and other regional institutions.
- **Type:** Academic Network
- **Access Level:** Through institutional partnerships

## Key Findings

### Strengths by Country:

- **Kazakhstan**: Strongest infrastructure with Nazarbayev University, Al-Farabi University, and extensive olympiad systems (IZhO, Daryn Center, Nazarbayev Intellectual Schools)
- **Uzbekistan**: Well-developed research infrastructure through Academy of Sciences institutes (Nuclear Physics, Ion-Plasma and Laser Technologies, Astronomy)
- **Kyrgyzstan**: Physics Hub provides innovative online platform for physics education and olympiad preparation
- **Georgia**: Experimentorium Science Museum provides unique public engagement with physics; strong university research programs
- **Armenia**: Exceptional astrophysics research at Byurakan Observatory; strong laser physics research at Institute for Physical Research
- **Azerbaijan**: Active international collaborations through Baku State University and ANAS Institute of Physics

### Access Patterns:

- Most university programs require competitive national entrance examinations
- Research institutions offer access through university partnerships and postgraduate programs
- Olympiads provide pathways for gifted students to access advanced physics education
- Online platforms (Physics Hub) provide free access to physics learning resources
