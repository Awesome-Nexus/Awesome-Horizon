# Physics Resources in Bosnia and Herzegovina

## Executive Summary

Bosnia and Herzegovina has a rich tradition in physics education and research, with established university departments, active research institutions, and growing international collaborations. The country's physics community is centered around major universities in Sarajevo, Banja Luka, and Tuzla, supported by the Academy of Sciences and Arts. Recent developments include a landmark cooperation agreement with CERN and the development of the Sarajevo Ion Accelerator (SARAI) project.

---

## 1. University of Sarajevo - Department of Physics

### Overview
The Department of Physics at the Faculty of Natural Sciences and Mathematics (PMF) of the University of Sarajevo is the oldest and most prestigious physics department in Bosnia and Herzegovina, with the longest tradition in the country.

### History
- **Founded**: 1960 (as part of the newly established Faculty of Science, separated from the Faculty of Philosophy)
- **Location**: Zmaja od Bosne 33-35, 71000 Sarajevo
- **Contact**: +387 (033) 279 946 | fizika@pmf.unsa.ba
- **Website**: https://fizika.pmf.unsa.ba/eng/

| First Cycle | Bachelor in Physics Education | 4 years | 240 |
| Second Cycle | Master in Physics | 1 year | 60 |

## 5. Academy of Sciences and Arts

#### History
