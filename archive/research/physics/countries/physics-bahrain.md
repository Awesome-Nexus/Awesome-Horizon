# Physics Education and Research in Bahrain

## Overview

The Kingdom of Bahrain has a developing physics education and research ecosystem centered primarily around its universities, with the **University of Bahrain** being the only institution offering dedicated academic programs in physics. The country emphasizes applied physics research aligned with national priorities such as renewable energy, medical physics, and materials science.

| Attribute | Details |

| **Focus** | Health sciences, medical education, and related fields |

While AGU does not offer a dedicated physics degree program, physics-related education is integrated into:

Recent developments (February 2025):
- Upgraded Diagnostic Radiology Department with AI-powered equipment
- First of its kind in the Middle East featuring AI-enabled MRI machines, CT scanners, and digital X-rays

## 4. Physics Education in Schools

### 4.1 Secondary Education System

The Kingdom of Bahrain offers multiple curriculum options for physics education at the secondary level:

| Curriculum Type | Physics Coverage | Examination |
|----------------|------------------|-------------|
| **Bahrain National Curriculum (Tawjihiya)** | Comprehensive physics curriculum | Tawjihiya examination |
| **British Curriculum** | Physics as part of Science (GCSE/IGCSE, A-Levels) | GCSE, A-Level exams |
| **American Curriculum** | Physics in Grades 9-12 | SAT, AP Physics |
| **International Baccalaureate (IB)** | IB Physics (SL/HL) | IB examinations |
| **CBSE (Indian)** | Physics, Chemistry, Mathematics | Board exams |
| **French Curriculum** | Physics and Chemistry (collège/lycée) | Baccalauréat |

### 4.2 Science Olympiads and Competitions

| Competition | Description | Organizer |
|-------------|-------------|-----------|
| **Gulf Science Olympiad** | Regional competition in mathematics, physics, and chemistry | Arab Bureau of Education for the Gulf States |
| **GCC Physics Olympiad** | Gulf Cooperation Council physics competition | GCC Education Ministries |
| **Science Meister** | National quiz competition covering physics, chemistry, biology | Open to all Bahrain schools |
| **World Robot Olympiad** | Robotics and physics applications | International |
