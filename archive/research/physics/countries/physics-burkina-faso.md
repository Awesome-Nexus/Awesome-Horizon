# Physics Institutions in Burkina Faso

## Overview

Burkina Faso has a developing physics research and education infrastructure centered around its public universities and national research centers. The country is an active member of the West African Physical Society (SOAPHYS) and participates in various international collaborations, particularly in climate science, space physics, and renewable energy research.

---

## 1. National Physics Societies and Associations

| Institution | Name (French) | Year Established | Description | Contact |
|-------------|---------------|------------------|-------------|---------|
| **West African Physical Society (SOAPHYS)** | Société Ouest Africaine de Physique | 2017 | Regional scientific association headquartered in Ouagadougou, Burkina Faso. Brings together physicists from 7+ West African countries including Burkina Faso, Benin, Côte d'Ivoire, Mali, Niger, Senegal, and Togo. Publishes the Journal of Physics of SOAPHYS (JPS). | www.soaphys.org, secretariat@soaphys.org |
| **African Physical Society** | - | - | Burkina Faso is a member country through its national representatives | www.africanphysicalsociety.org |

### SOAPHYS Details
- **Founded**: July 27, 2017 at Université Ouaga I Pr. Joseph Ki Zerbo
- **Member Countries**: Benin, Burkina Faso, Côte d'Ivoire, Mali, Niger, Senegal, Togo, and Mauritania
- **Mission**: Regional integration, scientific diplomacy, and valorization of physics research in West Africa
- **Publications**: Journal de Physique de la SOAPHYS (JPS)
- **Congress**: Holds biennial congresses (2019 in Ouagadougou, 2023, 2025 in Benin)

**Key Laboratories at UJKZ:**

| Laboratory | Acronym | Department | Research Areas |
|------------|---------|------------|----------------|
| Laboratoire de Matériaux et Environnement | LAME | Physics | Materials, environment, first PhD training in Physics in Ouagadougou |
| Laboratoire de Physique et Chimie de l'Environnement | LPCE | Physics | Environmental physics, climate |
| Laboratoire des Matériaux, Héliophysique et Environnement | LaMHE | UFR/SEA | Heliophysics, space weather, materials |

**Research Teams at LAME:**
- **Plasma and Energy Team (PEnerg)**: Plasma in electrical power devices, plasma-environment interaction, solar energy, alternative energy
- **Heterogeneous Environment and Arid Environment Team (MHEA)**: Climate change, water and sanitation, soil physics
- **Matter and Radiation Team (MatRay)**: Ionizing/non-ionizing radiation, gamma spectrometry, materials science, space meteorology

**Notable Faculty:**
- Prof. Frédéric Ouattara - Former Minister of Higher Education (2022), space physics specialist, BurkinaSat-1 leader
- Dr. Sidiki Zongo - Nonlinear optics, hybrid-perovskite materials for solar cells
- Prof. Jean Koulidiati - Renewable energy, solar cookers
- Prof. Antoine Béré - Materials science, thermal physics
