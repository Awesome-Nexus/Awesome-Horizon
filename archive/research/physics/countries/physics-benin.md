# Physics Institutions in Benin

## Overview

Benin has a developing physics research ecosystem centered primarily around the **University of Abomey-Calavi (UAC)**, the country's largest and oldest university. The nation has made significant strides in mathematical physics and atmospheric physics research through institutions like the ICMPA-UNESCO Chair and various university departments.

## Summary

Benin's physics research landscape is anchored by the University of Abomey-Calavi and led internationally by the ICMPA-UNESCO Chair under Professor Mahouton Hounkonnou. The country has established itself as a regional hub for mathematical physics in Africa through the COPROMAPH workshop series and extensive international partnerships spanning Europe, North America, and across Africa. Key research strengths include mathematical physics, atmospheric physics, and environmental physics applications.
