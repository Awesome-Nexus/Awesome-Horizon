# Physics Institutions in West Africa

## Executive Summary

This comprehensive report documents physics institutions, societies, research centers, and notable physicists across seven West African countries: Nigeria, Ghana, Senegal, Ivory Coast (Côte d'Ivoire), Burkina Faso, Mali, and Niger. The region has a growing physics community with active national societies, international collaborations (particularly with CERN, ICTP, and IAEA), and increasing research output in areas such as atmospheric physics, nuclear physics, materials science, and renewable energy.
