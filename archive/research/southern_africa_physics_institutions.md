### Major Research Themes Across the Region

1. **Astronomy and Space Science**: SKA participation (South Africa, Botswana, Mozambique, Namibia)
2. **Renewable Energy**: Solar energy research (Malawi, Zambia, Zimbabwe)
3. **Environmental Physics**: Climate change, oceanography (Mozambique, Namibia, Botswana)
4. **Medical Physics**: Radiation physics, nuclear medicine (Zambia, Zimbabwe, South Africa)
5. **Materials Science**: Ion beam analysis, nanotechnology (Namibia, South Africa, Botswana)
