# Major Physics Research Centers in India

## Comprehensive Research Compilation

This document provides detailed information about major physics research centers in India, organized by category.

## 1. Department of Atomic Energy (DAE) Institutions

The **Department of Atomic Energy (DAE)** is an Indian government department established in 1954 under the direct charge of the Prime Minister. It oversees India's nuclear energy and radiation technology programs.

| Aspect | Details |
|--------|---------|
| **Established** | 3 August 1954 |
| **Headquarters** | Mumbai, Maharashtra |
| **First Minister** | Jawaharlal Nehru |
| **First Secretary** | Homi J. Bhabha |
| **Annual Budget** | ₹25,078.49 crore (US$3.0 billion) FY2023-24 |
| **Website** | [dae.gov.in](https://dae.gov.in/) |

### DAE Research & Development Centers

| Institution | Location | Specialization |
|-------------|----------|----------------|
| Bhabha Atomic Research Centre (BARC) | Mumbai, Maharashtra | Multidisciplinary nuclear research |
| Indira Gandhi Centre for Atomic Research (IGCAR) | Kalpakkam, Tamil Nadu | Fast breeder reactors |
| Raja Ramanna Centre for Advanced Technology (RRCAT) | Indore, Madhya Pradesh | Lasers, accelerators |
| Variable Energy Cyclotron Centre (VECC) | Kolkata, West Bengal | Cyclotrons, nuclear physics |
| Global Centre for Nuclear Energy Partnership (GCNEP) | Bahadurgarh, Haryana | Nuclear energy partnership |

### DAE-Aided Basic Science Institutions

| Institution | Location | Research Focus |
|-------------|----------|----------------|
| Tata Institute of Fundamental Research (TIFR) | Mumbai, Maharashtra | Fundamental physics, astronomy |
| Saha Institute of Nuclear Physics (SINP) | Kolkata, West Bengal | Nuclear physics, biophysics |
| Institute of Physics (IOP) | Bhubaneswar, Odisha | Condensed matter, neutrino physics |
| Harish-Chandra Research Institute (HRI) | Prayagraj, Uttar Pradesh | Theoretical physics, mathematics |
| National Institute of Science Education and Research (NISER) | Jatani, Odisha | Basic sciences education |
| Institute for Plasma Research (IPR) | Gandhinagar, Gujarat | Plasma physics |
| Institute of Mathematical Sciences (IMSc) | Chennai, Tamil Nadu | Theoretical physics, mathematics |

| Area | Description |
|------|-------------|

| **Material Sciences** | Metallurgy, radiation damage studies, nanomaterials |
| **Chemical Engineering** | Reprocessing, thorium fuel cycle, desalination |

| **Physics Research** | Condensed matter, nuclear physics, astrophysics |
| **Electronics & Instrumentation** | Reactor control systems, radiation detectors |
| **Life Sciences** | Radiation biology, cancer research, crop improvement |

| **Location** | New Delhi |
| **Type** | Inter-University Centre (IUC) |
| **Parent Organization** | University Grants Commission (UGC) |
| **Director** | Prof. Avinash Chandra Pandey |
| **Website** | [iuac.res.in](https://www.iuac.res.in/) |

### Accelerator Facilities

| Facility | Specifications |
|----------|---------------|
| **15 UD Pelletron** | Tandem accelerator up to 15 MV terminal potential |
| **Superconducting Linear Accelerator** | Niobium QWR-based LINAC booster |
| **1.7 MV Pelletron (RBS-AMS)** | Rutherford Backscattering & AMS |
| **Accelerator Mass Spectrometry (AMS)** | Detection of 10Be and 26Al |
| **Ion Implanter** | 30-200 keV ion beams |
| **Tabletop Ion Accelerators** | 30 kV and 60 kV (for education) |
| **High Current Injector (HCI)** | RFQ-DTL based heavy ion injector |
| **Free Electron Laser (DLS)** | Delhi Light Source (under construction) |

### Research Areas

| **Materials Science** | Ion beam modification, nanopatterning |
| **Nuclear Physics** | Nuclear reactions, structure studies |
| **Atomic Physics** | Ion-atom collisions |
| **Radiation Biology** | Biological effects of radiation |
| **Accelerator Mass Spectrometry** | Dating, environmental tracers |
| **Geochronology** | Rock dating, geological studies |

### Academic Programs

| Program | Description |
|---------|-------------|
