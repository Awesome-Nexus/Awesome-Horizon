# Physics Institutions in Baltic Countries

## Overview
This document provides comprehensive information about physics institutions, societies, universities, research institutes, and notable physicists in Estonia, Latvia, and Lithuania.

### Physics Nobel Prize Connections

While no physicist from the Baltic states has won the Nobel Prize in Physics, Baltic researchers have contributed to Nobel-winning research:
- Baltic scientists participated in the Higgs boson discovery (2013 Nobel Prize), contributing to CMS and ATLAS experiments at CERN
