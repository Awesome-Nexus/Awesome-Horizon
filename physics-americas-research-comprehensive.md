## United States

### Universities with Free Online Courses

#### MIT OpenCourseWare (OCW)
- **Country:** United States
- **Type:** University Open Educational Resource
- **URL:** https://ocw.mit.edu/search/
- **Description:** MIT OpenCourseWare is a web-based publication of virtually all MIT course content, including extensive physics courses. OCW is open and available to the world and is a permanent MIT activity. Offers complete course materials including lecture notes, assignments, and exams for undergraduate and graduate physics courses.

#### Stanford University
- **Country:** United States
- **Type:** University
- **URL:** https://www.stanford.edu/
- **Description:** Leading research university with strong physics programs. Stanford offers online physics courses through various platforms and has produced significant physics research. Home to 6,699 inventions created by federally funded research and 400+ start-up companies. Features Nobel laureates among faculty.

#### UC Berkeley Physics
- **Country:** United States
- **Type:** University
- **URL:** https://www.physics.berkeley.edu/
- **Description:** Ranked #1 public research university with 12 Nobel laureates on faculty (including John Clarke, 2025 Nobel Prize in Physics winner). Over 300 degree programs. The Physics Department at Berkeley is located at 366 Physics North, Berkeley, CA 94720.

#### Caltech (California Institute of Technology)
- **Country:** United States
- **Type:** University/Research Institute
- **URL:** https://www.caltech.edu/
- **Description:** World-renowned science and engineering institute. Operates LIGO Laboratory in collaboration with MIT. Fourth-year undergraduate Akshar Ramkumar (2026) was awarded the Churchill Scholarship. Home to breakthrough research in physics, planetary science, and quantum computing.

#### Harvard University Physics
- **Country:** United States
- **Type:** University
- **URL:** https://www.physics.harvard.edu/
- **Description:** Premier physics department with extensive research programs and educational resources.

#### University of Colorado Boulder Physics
- **Country:** United States
- **Type:** University
- **URL:** https://www.colorado.edu/physics/
- **Description:** Features 4 Nobel Prizes in Physics since 2001, $84 million in research expenditures in 2024, ranked #1 best public university in atomic, molecular and optical physics (2023 US News & World Report), awards approximately 100 undergraduate degrees annually.

#### LIGO Laboratory
- **Country:** United States
- **Type:** Research Observatory
- **URL:** https://www.ligo.caltech.edu/
- **Description:** The Laser Interferometer Gravitational-Wave Observatory consists of two installations in Hanford, Washington and Livingston, Louisiana. Operated by Caltech and MIT consortium, funded by the National Science Foundation. International resource for physics and astrophysics research. Recently completed fourth observing run and released GWTC-4.0 gravitational-wave catalog.

### Open Online Learning Platforms

#### edX Physics Courses
- **Country:** United States
- **Type:** Online Learning Platform
- **URL:** https://www.edx.org/learn/physics
- **Description:** Online platform offering physics courses from top universities. Covers topics including: Mechanics (classical and quantum), Optics, Thermodynamics, Electromagnetism, Relativity, and Acoustics. Offers beginner-friendly courses and advanced certificate programs. Provides structured learning paths for career development in physics.

#### The Physics Classroom
- **Country:** United States
- **Type:** Educational Website
- **URL:** https://www.physicsclassroom.com/
- **Description:** Free online physics tutorials written by teachers for teachers and students. Features interactive simulations (Interactives), Concept Builders for solidifying concepts, Minds On challenges, Calc Pad for equation practice, and Science Reasoning exercises. Offers free curriculum for Conceptual, Algebra-Based, and Honors Physics. Task Tracker LMS available for teachers.

#### Khan Academy Physics
- **Country:** United States
- **Type:** Online Educational Platform
- **URL:** https://www.khanacademy.org/science/physics
- **Description:** Free comprehensive physics education covering all major topics from high school to early college level. Features video lessons, practice exercises, and personalized learning dashboards.

#### Physics Forums
- **Country:** United States
- **Type:** Online Community/Forum
- **URL:** https://www.physicsforums.com/
- **Description:** Active online community for physics discussion with over 800,000 registered members. Sections include: Homework Help (step-by-step assistance), Science Education and Careers, Physics (classical, quantum, high energy), Astronomy and Cosmology, Mathematics, and Engineering.

## Canada

### Universities

#### University of Toronto Physics
- **Country:** Canada
- **Type:** University
- **URL:** https://www.physics.utoronto.ca/
- **Description:** Leading Canadian physics department. Features research areas including quantum computing (Prof. Aephraim Steinberg), atmospheric science, and atomic clock breakthroughs. Home to Ph.D. student research on snow cover observations and atomic clock technology.

#### Perimeter Institute for Theoretical Physics
- **Country:** Canada
- **Type:** Research Institute
- **URL:** https://perimeterinstitute.ca/
- **Description:** World-leading center for theoretical physics dedicated to unraveling the deepest mysteries of the universe. 120,000 square foot facility home to over 250 researchers. Founded with vision of fostering collaboration in theoretical physics. Features free educational resources for grades 5-12, public lectures, and the PIOutreach YouTube channel. Endorsed by Stephen Hawking as Distinguished Research Chair.

#### Perimeter Institute Teacher Resources
- **Country:** Canada
- **Type:** Educational Resource
- **URL:** https://resources.perimeterinstitute.ca/
- **Description:** Free digital resources designed to help teachers explain physics and science topics for grades 5-12. Includes lectures, videos, and classroom activities making physics accessible from elementary to graduate level.

#### McGill University Physics
- **Country:** Canada
- **Type:** University
- **URL:** https://www.physics.mcgill.ca/
- **Description:** Active research department with regular seminars and colloquia. Features research in gravitational waves, quantum field theory, cosmology, and astrophysics. Recent PhD defense by Dr. Nayyer Raza on machine learning classification of gravitational-wave events.

#### University of Waterloo Physics and Astronomy
- **Country:** Canada
- **Type:** University
- **URL:** https://uwaterloo.ca/physics-astronomy/
- **Description:** Home to Nobel Laureate Donna Strickland. Features Waterloo Centre for Astrophysics and Institute for Quantum Computing. Works closely with neighboring Perimeter Institute. Offers co-op placements and hands-on research opportunities. Combines rigorous academics with flexible pathways for careers in science, technology, medicine, and data science.

#### University of British Columbia Physics & Astronomy
- **Country:** Canada
- **Type:** University
- **URL:** https://phas.ubc.ca/
- **Description:** Comprehensive physics and astronomy department offering undergraduate and graduate programs with active research in multiple physics subfields.

#### Simon Fraser University (SFU) Physics
- **Country:** Canada
- **Type:** University
- **URL:** https://www.sfu.ca/physics.html
- **Description:** Pushes boundaries of scientific discovery and technological innovation. Provides exceptional training at all levels with personalized mentoring and hands-on research opportunities. Actively engages with diverse communities to share the wonders of the physical universe.

#### Canadian Institute for Theoretical Astrophysics (CITA)
- **Country:** Canada
- **Type:** Research Institute
- **URL:** https://www.cita.utoronto.ca/
- **Description:** Nationally supported research centre at University of Toronto devoted to studying the origin and evolution of the universe. Fosters interaction within the astrophysics community through workshops and visitors. Celebrating 40th Anniversary in 2026 (May 27-29). Home to renowned planetary scientist Sara Seager (North Star Distinguished Professor).

## Brazil

### Universities and Research Centers

#### ICTP South American Institute for Fundamental Research (ICTP-SAIFR)
- **Country:** Brazil
- **Type:** Research Institute
- **URL:** https://www.ictp-saifr.org/
- **Description:** South American regional center in São Paulo created through collaboration of ICTP, Instituto de Física Teórica (IFT) of UNESP, and FAPESP. Offers seminars, minicourses, schools and workshops for graduate students and researchers in theoretical physics. Provides funding for short-term and long-term visitors as well as postdoctoral and permanent researchers. Features extensive São Paulo International Schools on Theoretical Physics throughout 2026.

#### Brazilian Center for Research in Physics (CBPF)
- **Country:** Brazil
- **Type:** Research Institute
- **URL:** http://wwwold.cbpf.br/en-us/home/about-cbpf
- **Description:** Institute of the Ministry of Science, Technology and Innovation of Brazil. Founded in 1949 by eminent Brazilian scientists including César Lattes, Jaime Tiomno. One of the most important physics research institutions in Brazil. Graduate program ranks among the best in the country. Located in Urca neighborhood of Rio de Janeiro.

#### Instituto de Física Teórica (IFT) - UNESP
- **Country:** Brazil
- **Type:** Research Institute/University Department
- **URL:** https://www.ift.unesp.br/
- **Description:** Institute of Theoretical Physics at São Paulo State University (UNESP). Collaborating institution with ICTP-SAIFR. Located at R. Dr. Bento Teobaldo Ferraz, 271 - Barra-Funda, São Paulo.

#### Federal University of Rio de Janeiro (UFRJ)
- **Country:** Brazil
- **Type:** University
- **URL:** https://www.ufrj.br/
- **Description:** Major Brazilian federal university offering physics programs. Features Coppe (Graduate Engineering Program) with research in transport engineering and physics applications. Offers over 200 graduate positions in stricto sensu programs.

#### Universidade Estadual de Campinas (UNICAMP)
- **Country:** Brazil
- **Type:** University
- **URL:** https://www.unicamp.br/
- **Description:** Leading Brazilian public university with strong physics research programs and graduate education.

## National Laboratories

### United States National Laboratories

#### Brookhaven National Laboratory (BNL)
- **Country:** United States
- **Type:** National Laboratory
- **URL:** https://www.bnl.gov/
- **Description:** Advances fundamental research in nuclear and particle physics. Applies photon sciences and nanomaterials research to energy challenges. Performs cross-disciplinary research on computation, sustainable energy, national security, and Earth's ecosystems. Home to NSLS-II synchrotron light source and Center for Functional Nanomaterials (CFN).

#### Oak Ridge National Laboratory (ORNL)
- **Country:** United States
- **Type:** National Laboratory
- **URL:** https://www.ornl.gov/
- **Description:** More than 7,000 scientists, engineers, technicians from 70+ nations. 1,132 patents issued since 2010. 2,000+ scientific publications per year. Provides access to unique facilities drawing thousands of visiting researchers annually. Led by Director Stephen K. Streiffer.

#### Los Alamos National Laboratory (LANL)
- **Country:** United States
- **Type:** National Laboratory
- **URL:** https://www.lanl.gov/
- **Description:** One of the world's most innovative multidisciplinary research institutions with almost 80 years of science and innovation. 18,000+ people in New Mexico and beyond. Features Bradbury Science Museum and Manhattan Project National Historic Park. $2.6 million donated to nonprofits in 2023 Employee Giving Campaign.

#### Fermi National Accelerator Laboratory (Fermilab)
- **Country:** United States
- **Type:** National Laboratory
- **URL:** https://www.fnal.gov/
- **Description:** America's premier particle physics laboratory. Research includes particle physics, accelerator science, and quantum science. Collaborates on TOTEM experiment at CERN's LHC. Features public events and educational programs.

#### Lawrence Livermore National Laboratory (LLNL)
- **Country:** United States
- **Type:** National Laboratory
- **URL:** https://www.llnl.gov/
- **Description:** More than 70 years of applying science and technology. Focus areas: Nuclear Deterrence, Energy Security, Threat Preparedness and Response, Multi-Domain Deterrence. Home to world's largest laser system (National Ignition Facility). Over 1,132 patents issued.

#### Sandia National Laboratories
- **Country:** United States
- **Type:** National Laboratory
- **URL:** https://www.sandia.gov/
- **Description:** 75 years delivering science and technology for national security. Unique responsibilities in nuclear weapons program. Solves complex national security problems through science-based systems engineering. Features self-guided virtual tours.

#### NASA Glenn Research Center
- **Country:** United States
- **Type:** National Laboratory/Research Center
- **URL:** https://www.nasa.gov/glenn/
- **Description:** Transforms aviation and revolutionizes space exploration. Home to Neil Armstrong Test Facility in Sandusky, Ohio (6,700 acres) with world's largest space simulation test facilities. Publishes monthly "AeroSpace Frontiers" newsletter.

#### SLAC National Accelerator Laboratory
- **Country:** United States
- **Type:** National Laboratory
- **URL:** https://www.slac.stanford.edu/
- **Description:** Stanford Linear Accelerator Center - premier research facility for particle physics and photon science.

#### Argonne National Laboratory
- **Country:** United States
- **Type:** National Laboratory
- **URL:** https://www.anl.gov/
- **Description:** Multidisciplinary science and engineering research center.

## Physics Societies & Organizations

### United States

#### American Physical Society (APS)
- **Country:** United States
- **Type:** Professional Society
- **URL:** https://www.aps.org/
- **Description:** World's largest physics society with 50,000+ members worldwide. Publishes 19 leading peer-reviewed journals through Physical Review. Member of Purpose-Led Publishing coalition. Hosts annual APS Global Physics Summit. Provides career resources, networking, and science advocacy.

#### American Institute of Physics (AIP)
- **Country:** United States
- **Type:** Professional Organization
- **URL:** https://www.aip.org/
- **Description:** Federation of physical science societies. Sponsors Congressional Science Fellows program placing scientists in legislative offices. Partners with American Association of Physics Teachers (AAPT) and other member societies.

#### Society of Physics Students (SPS)
- **Country:** United States
- **Type:** Student Organization
- **URL:** https://www.spsnational.org/
- **Description:** Professional association for students and advisers. More than 800 chapters at colleges and universities across the United States. Open to anyone interested in physical sciences including astronomy, chemistry, computer science, and engineering majors. Builds skills in communication, leadership, networking, and research.

#### American Association of Physics Teachers (AAPT)
- **Country:** United States
- **Type:** Professional Society
- **URL:** https://www.aapt.org/
- **Description:** Located at 1 Physics Ellipse, College Park, MD. Professional organization for physics educators at all levels.

### Canada

#### Canadian Association of Physicists (CAP)
- **Country:** Canada
- **Type:** Professional Society
- **URL:** https://www.cap.ca/
- **Description:** Voice of Canadian physicists. Pursues initiatives enhancing physics and physicists' contributions in Canada. Leader of 2025 International Year of Quantum Science and Technology Team Canada group. Offers CAP Medals and Fellowships, annual Congress, and the Ignite the Spark Campaign supporting student initiatives.

### Regional Organizations

#### Organization of American States (OAS)
- **Country:** Regional (35 member states)
- **Type:** International Organization
- **URL:** https://www.oas.org/
- **Description:** World's oldest regional organization (established 1889-1890). 35 member states with 62 permanent observer states plus EU. Addresses science and technology, education, and development across the Americas.
