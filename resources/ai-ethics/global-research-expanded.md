## 2. RESPONSIBLE AI FRAMEWORKS

### Microsoft Responsible AI Principles
**URL:** https://docs.microsoft.com/en-us/learn/modules/responsible-ai-principles/

**6 Core Principles:**
1. **Fairness** - Treat everyone fairly
2. **Reliability & Safety** - Perform reliably and safely
3. **Privacy & Security** - Respect privacy and security
4. **Inclusiveness** - Empower everyone
5. **Transparency** - Be transparent
6. **Accountability** - Be accountable

### Google AI Principles
**URL:** https://ai.google/principles/

**7 AI Principles:**
1. Be socially beneficial
2. Avoid creating or reinforcing unfair bias
3. Be built and tested for safety
4. Be accountable to people
5. Incorporate privacy design principles
6. Uphold high standards of scientific excellence
7. Be made available for beneficial use
