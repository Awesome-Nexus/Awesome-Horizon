## 2.2 Helmholtz Association - Physics Research Centers

### Deutsches Elektronen-Synchrotron (DESY)
- **Locations**: Hamburg, Zeuthen (near Berlin)
- **Website**: https://www.desy.de/

- **Budget**: ~€359 million (2024)
- **Staff**: ~3,000 + 3,000 guest scientists annually
- **Research Areas**:
  - Particle and high energy physics
  - Photon science
  - Astroparticle physics
  - Accelerator physics
- **Major Facilities**:
  - **PETRA III**: 3rd-generation synchrotron light source
  - **FLASH**: Free-electron laser (soft X-rays)
  - **European XFEL**: World's most powerful X-ray laser (operated by DESY)
  - **HERA** (historic): First electron-proton collider
  - **PITZ**: Photoinjector test facility
