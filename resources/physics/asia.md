#### CERN Collaborations

China is a major collaborator with CERN:
- **ATLAS Experiment**: Multiple Chinese institutions participate, including Tsinghua University, Peking University, IHEP, Nanjing University, and USTC
- **CMS Experiment**: Strong Chinese participation with significant contributions to detector construction and data analysis
- **LHCb Experiment**: Chinese institutions including Tsinghua participate in flavor physics research
- **ALICE Experiment**: China contributes to heavy-ion physics research
- **China-CERN Workshop**: Annual collaboration meetings held since 1999
