## üî¨ MAJOR INTERNATIONAL FACILITIES

### Particle Physics
- **LHC** (CERN, Switzerland/France) - 13.6 TeV proton collisions
- **BESSY II** (HZB, Germany) - Synchrotron radiation
- **PETRA III** (DESY, Germany) - Hard X-ray source
- **SPring-8** (Japan) - 3rd-gen synchrotron
- **APS** (Argonne, USA) - Advanced Photon Source
- **SSRF** (Shanghai, China) - Synchrotron

### Neutron Sources
- **ILL** (Grenoble, France) - Most intense neutron source
- **SNS** (Oak Ridge, USA) - Spallation Neutron Source
- **ISIS** (RAL, UK) - Pulsed neutron source
- **SINQ** (PSI, Switzerland) - Continuous spallation source
- **J-PARC** (Japan) - Neutron and muon facility

### Lasers & Optics
- **European XFEL** (Germany) - Most powerful X-ray laser
- **LCLS** (SLAC, USA) - X-ray free-electron laser
- **ELI-NP** (Romania) - Extreme Light Infrastructure
- **NIF** (LLNL, USA) - Inertial confinement fusion

### Gravitational Waves
- **LIGO** (USA) - Laser interferometer gravitational observatory
- **Virgo** (Italy) - European gravitational wave detector
- **KAGRA** (Japan) - Underground gravitational wave detector

### Fusion Research
- **ITER** (France) - International fusion reactor
- **JET** (UK) - Joint European Torus
- **WEST** (France) - Tungsten tokamak
- **KSTAR** (Korea) - Superconducting tokamak
