## How to Use This Resource Collection

1. **For K-12 Teachers**: Start with Project Learning Tree, NOAA Education, and National Geographic Education for curriculum-aligned activities.

2. **For Undergraduate Students**: Combine MIT OpenCourseWare or Coursera courses with data analysis using Google Earth Engine or NASA Earthdata.

3. **For Researchers**: Access primary data through GBIF, NASA Earthdata, and IPCC. Publish findings in journals like Nature Climate Change or Environmental Science & Technology.

4. **For Policy Makers**: Reference UNEP, IPCC assessments, and country-specific agency resources for evidence-based decision making.

5. **For Lifelong Learners**: Explore Earth Day Network resources, NASA Climate Change portal, and open-access journals for continuous learning.
