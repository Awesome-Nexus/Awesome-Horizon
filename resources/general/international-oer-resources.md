**Website:** https://nptel.ac.in/

**Overview:**
- India's largest online learning platform for engineering and science

### SWAYAM (Study Webs of Active-Learning for Young Aspiring Minds)
**Website:** https://swayam.gov.in/

**Overview:**
- Government of India's official MOOC platform
- Launched by Ministry of Education
- Hosts 2,000+ courses from Class 9 to postgraduate level
- Credits transferable to academic records

**Key Features:**
- Four-quadrant approach: Video lectures, reading material, self-assessment, online discussion
- Courses from NCERT, NIOS, IGNOU, and universities
- 32 national coordinators
- Free access; paid certification

**STEAM Categories:**
- Engineering and Technology
- Science
- Mathematics
- Agriculture
- Architecture and Planning
- Computer Applications

**Notable Programs:**
- SWAYAM PRABHA: 34 DTH channels broadcasting educational content 24/7
- NPTEL courses integrated
- UGC-MOOCs for undergraduate credit transfer

## How to Use This Resource

1. **Identify your region/language preference** - Start with platforms that offer content in your preferred language
2. **Determine your learning goal** - Choose between full courses, textbooks, or supplementary materials
3. **Check for certification needs** - Some platforms offer free learning but charge for certificates
4. **Explore multiple platforms** - Different platforms may offer unique perspectives on the same subject
5. **Contribute back** - Many OER platforms welcome contributions and feedback
