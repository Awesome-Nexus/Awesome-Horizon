## 1. MAJOR MOOC PLATFORMS

### Coursera
- **URL:** https://www.coursera.org/
- **Description:** Leading online learning platform partnering with 350+ top universities and companies worldwide. Offers courses, Specializations, Professional Certificates, and full degree programs.
- **Key Features:**
  - Professional Certificates from Google, IBM, Meta, Adobe
  - Bachelor's and Master's degree programs
  - Coursera Plus subscription for unlimited access
  - 7-day free trials on most courses
  - Financial aid available
- **Subjects:** Business, Computer Science, Data Science, AI/ML, Cybersecurity, UX Design, Project Management, Health, Arts & Humanities, Physical Science, Social Sciences
- **Notable Programs:**
  - Google Data Analytics Professional Certificate
  - Google Project Management Professional Certificate
  - IBM Data Science Professional Certificate
  - Deep Learning Specialization (Andrew Ng)
  - Machine Learning Specialization
- **Pricing:** Free to audit; Certificates $39-$99/month; Coursera Plus $399/year

### edX
- **URL:** https://www.edx.org/
- **Description:** Online learning platform founded by Harvard and MIT, offering courses from 250+ institutions including Ivy League universities and Fortune 500 companies.
- **Key Features:**
  - MicroMasters programs (graduate-level credentials)

  - Full Master's and Bachelor's degrees
  - Executive Education programs
