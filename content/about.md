# About Awesome Horizon

Welcome to **Awesome Horizon**, a comprehensive curated learning platform designed to unlock your potential across STEAM disciplines.

## Our Mission

To democratize access to high-quality educational resources by providing a carefully curated collection of learning materials for students, educators, and lifelong learners across:

- **Science** - From fundamental physics to cutting-edge research
- **Technology** - Programming, AI, web development, and emerging tech
- **Engineering** - Civil, mechanical, electrical, and aerospace engineering
- **Arts** - Creative expression, design, history, and digital arts
- **Mathematics** - From basic algebra to advanced mathematical concepts

## Our Categories

### Science
Explore the natural world through physics, chemistry, biology, psychology, and environmental science. Features resources from MIT OpenCourseWare, Khan Academy, and leading educational institutions.

### Technology
Stay current with programming languages, computer science principles, web development, iOS development, and emerging technologies. Includes courses from Stanford, Harvard, and industry leaders.

### Engineering
Build the future with resources covering aerospace, civil, mechanical, electrical, and other engineering disciplines. Learn from top universities and industry professionals.

### Arts
Express creativity through digital photography, industrial design, painting, drawing, and art history. Combine technical skills with artistic vision.

### Mathematics
Master the language of the universe from basic algebra to advanced calculus, linear algebra, and statistics. Features comprehensive courses and interactive tools.

## Built With Modern Technology

**Awesome Horizon** is built using:
- **Nuxt 3** - The intuitive Vue framework for modern web applications
- **TailwindCSS** - Utility-first CSS framework for rapid UI development
- **Nuxt Content** - File-based CMS for seamless content management
- **Vue 3** - Progressive JavaScript framework for building user interfaces
