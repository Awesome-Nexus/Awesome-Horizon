The Clay Mathematics Institute offers **$1 million** for solutions to each:
1. P vs NP
2. Hodge Conjecture
3. Riemann Hypothesis
4. Yang-Mills Existence and Mass Gap
5. Navier-Stokes Existence and Smoothness

## Notable Mathematicians

### Historical Giants
- **Euclid** (~300 BCE) - "Elements," geometry foundation
- **Archimedes** (~287-212 BCE) - Calculus precursors, π approximation
- **Isaac Newton** (1643-1727) - Calculus, physics
- **Leonhard Euler** (1707-1783) - Most prolific, e^(iπ) + 1 = 0
- **Carl Friedrich Gauss** (1777-1855) - "Prince of Mathematicians"
- **Évariste Galois** (1811-1832) - Group theory, died at 20

### Modern Era
- **Srinivasa Ramanujan** (1887-1920) - Self-taught genius, notebooks
- **David Hilbert** (1862-1943) - 23 problems shaped 20th century
- **Kurt Gödel** (1906-1978) - Incompleteness theorems
- **Alexander Grothendieck** (1928-2014) - Algebraic geometry revolution
- **John Conway** (1937-2020) - Game of Life, monster group
- **Andrew Wiles** (1953-) - Proved Fermat's Last Theorem (1995)
- **Grigori Perelman** (1966-) - Poincaré conjecture, declined Fields Medal

### Contemporary Stars
- **Terence Tao** (1975-) - "Mozart of Math," Fields 2006
- **Maryam Mirzakhani** (1977-2017) - First female Fields Medalist
- **Karen Uhlenbeck** (1942-) - First woman Abel Prize
- **Maryna Viazovska** (1984-) - Sphere packing, Fields 2022

## Open Problems & Research Frontiers

### Millennium Problems (Unsolved)
1. **Riemann Hypothesis** - Distribution of prime numbers
2. **P vs NP** - Computational complexity
3. **Navier-Stokes** - Fluid dynamics equations
4. **Yang-Mills Mass Gap** - Quantum physics
5. **Hodge Conjecture** - Algebraic geometry
6. **Birch and Swinnerton-Dyer** - Elliptic curves

### Other Famous Open Problems
- **Goldbach Conjecture** - Every even number > 2 is sum of two primes
- **Twin Prime Conjecture** - Infinitely many twin primes
- **Collatz Conjecture** - 3n+1 problem
- **abc Conjecture** - Mochizuki claimed proof (2012), still debated

### Recent Breakthroughs (2020-2026)
- **AlphaGeometry** (2024) - DeepMind AI solves geometry olympiad problems
- **Guth-Maynard** (2024) - Progress on Riemann Hypothesis
- **Viazovska** (2022) - Sphere packing in 8 and 24 dimensions
