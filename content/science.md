## Physics

### Introduction to Physics
- [Map of Physics](https://youtu.be/ZihywtixUYo) - Everything we know about physics - and a few things we don't - in a simple map by Dominic Walliman.
- [What is science?](https://youtu.be/TclBJZuUAOY) - A brief description of the nature of science, including examples and traits of good scientists.
- [HyperPhysics](http://hyperphysics.phy-astr.gsu.edu/hbase/hph.html) - Comprehensive coverage on most physics topics through online tutorials by the Department of Physics and Astronomy at Georgia State University.
- [Khan Academy Physics](https://www.khanacademy.org/science/physics) - Free physics courses covering mechanics, thermodynamics, electricity and magnetism.

### Physics Textbooks
- [Feynman Lectures on Physics](https://www.feynmanlectures.caltech.edu/) - The complete lectures by Richard Feynman, available free online.
- [OpenStax College Physics](https://openstax.org/details/books/college-physics-2e) - Free digital algebra-based physics textbook from OpenStax by Rice University.
- [OpenStax University Physics](https://openstax.org/details/books/university-physics-volume-1) - Free calculus-based physics textbook for university students.

### AP & College Physics
- [Flipping Physics AP](https://www.flippingphysics.com/ap-physics-1.html) - Collection of videos exploring concepts and example problems for every topic in AP Physics.
- [The Physics Classroom](https://www.physicsclassroom.com/class) - Videos, lessons, and simulations on topics in AP Physics.
- [Bozeman Physics AP](https://www.bozemanscience.com/ap-physics-1-video-list) - Video lessons on topics in AP Physics by Bozeman Science.

### Advanced Physics
- [MIT Classical Mechanics](https://ocw.mit.edu/courses/8-01sc-classical-mechanics-fall-2016/) - Full course on classical mechanics by MIT OpenCourseWare.
- [MIT Electricity & Magnetism](https://ocw.mit.edu/courses/8-02-physics-ii-electricity-and-magnetism-spring-2007/) - Second semester freshmen-level course of introductory physics.
- [MIT Thermodynamics](https://ocw.mit.edu/courses/5-60-thermodynamics-kinetics-spring-2008/) - Full course on thermodynamics by MIT OpenCourseWare.

## Chemistry

### General Chemistry
- [CrashCourse Chemistry](https://www.youtube.com/playlist?list=PL8dPuuaLjXtPHzzYuWy6fYEaX9mQQ8oGr) - Video playlist based on the AP Chemistry curriculum that explains concepts in short videos.
- [LibreTexts General Chemistry](https://chem.libretexts.org/Bookshelves/General_Chemistry) - Several free digital textbooks and textmaps on general chemistry.
- [Tyler DeWitt Chemistry](https://www.youtube.com/user/tdewitt451/featured) - Videos on almost every topic in chemistry targeted for students studying for AP and standardized exams.
- [Science Geek AP Chemistry](https://www.sciencegeek.net/APchemistry/index.shtml) - Self-directed student practice with PowerPoints, notes, reviews, and interactive quizzes.

### Organic Chemistry
- [Master Organic Chemistry](https://www.masterorganicchemistry.com/) - Comprehensive organic chemistry tutorials and practice problems.
- [Organic Chemistry Tutor](https://www.organicchemistrytutor.com/organic-chemistry-notes/) - Video tutorials and notes for organic chemistry concepts.

### Chemistry Simulations
- [3DMolecules](https://apps.apple.com/app/id1639183447) - An interactive 3D molecule simulation designed to immerse students in chemistry.
- [PhET Chemistry Simulations](https://phet.colorado.edu/en/simulations/filter?subjects=chemistry&type=html&sort=alpha&view=grid) - Interactive simulations covering common laboratory experiments and particle models.

## Biology

### General Biology

- [AP Biology Fiveable](https://library.fiveable.me/ap-bio) - On-demand lecture videos, study guides, trivia games and many more resources for AP Biology.

## Environmental Science

### AP Environmental Science
- [Khan Academy APES](https://www.khanacademy.org/science/ap-college-environmental-science) - Articles, videos, and practice problems on APES by Khan Academy.
- [Bozeman Science APES](https://www.bozemanscience.com/ap-environmental-science) - Video lessons on topics in APES by Bozeman Science.

## Astronomy & Space

### Space Science
- [NASA Space Place](https://spaceplace.nasa.gov) - NASA's educational website for students and educators.
- [NASA JPL Education](https://www.jpl.nasa.gov/edu/learn/tag/search/space+travel) - Educational resources from NASA's Jet Propulsion Laboratory.
- [Scale of the Universe](https://scaleofuniverse.com/) - Interactive website to explore the size and scale of objects relative to each other.
- [ESA Education](https://www.esa.int/Education) - Educational resources from the European Space Agency.

### Interactive Simulations
- [PhET Physics Simulations](https://phet.colorado.edu/en/simulations/filter?subjects=physics&type=html,prototype) - Interactive physics simulations for physics topics across different grade levels.
- [oPhysics Interactive](https://ophysics.com/index.html) - Growing collection of interactive physics simulations for every physics topic.
- [The Physics Aviary](https://www.thephysicsaviary.com) - Interactive physics simulations for topics in AP Physics 1 and 2.
