## üéØ Study Pathways

### For High School Students
1. **Start with:** Khan Academy Chemistry or OpenStax Chemistry
2. **Practice with:** PhET simulations and ChemCollective virtual labs
3. **Video support:** Tyler DeWitt, CrashCourse Chemistry
4. **Competition prep:** IChO preparatory problems (appropriate level)

### For Undergraduate Students
1. **General Chemistry:** MIT 5.111, LibreTexts
2. **Organic Chemistry:** Yale Freshman Organic Chemistry, The Organic Chemistry Tutor
3. **Physical Chemistry:** MIT 5.60 series, NPTEL courses
4. **Lab skills:** ChemCollective virtual labs, MIT Lab Chemistry

### For Graduate Students
1. **Advanced Topics:** MIT OCW graduate courses
2. **Computational Chemistry:** Q-Chem resources, GitHub repositories
3. **Research:** RCSB PDB, PubChem, primary literature
4. **Specialized:** EdX professional certificates

### For Self-Learners
1. **Structured path:** Coursera/edX specializations
2. **Free option:** MIT OCW + Khan Academy + LibreTexts
3. **Hands-on:** PhET + ChemCollective virtual labs
4. **Community:** ACS, RSC local sections
