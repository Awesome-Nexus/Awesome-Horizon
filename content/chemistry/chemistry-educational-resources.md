## 10. How to Use This Guide

1. **For Students**: Identify your level (K-12, Undergraduate, Graduate) and subject area to find appropriate resources.

2. **For Teachers**: Explore the virtual labs, PhET simulations, and RSC/ChemCollective resources for classroom activities.

3. **For Researchers**: Utilize the spectral databases (SDBS, NMRShiftDB) and crystallographic databases (CSD, PDB) for structural analysis.

4. **For Self-Learners**: Start with OpenStax or LibreTexts textbooks, supplement with MIT OCW or NPTEL lectures, and practice with simulation tools.
